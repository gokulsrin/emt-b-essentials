## Text

<u>Agonal breathing</u> — irregular, gasping respirations — is {{c1::not effective ventilation::effectiveness}}; treat the patient as {{c2::in cardiac arrest::clinical status}}.

## Extra

Agonal breaths are brainstem-driven gasps seen in the first minutes of cardiac arrest. Do not mistake them for adequate breathing — begin CPR immediately.
