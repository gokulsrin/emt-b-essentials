## Text

Use <b>head-tilt-chin-lift</b> for {{c1::non-trauma::trauma status}} patients; use <b>jaw-thrust</b> when {{c2::cervical spine injury is suspected::indication}}.

## Extra

The jaw-thrust opens the airway by displacing the mandible anteriorly without extending the neck. If jaw-thrust alone fails to open the airway in a trauma patient, a head-tilt-chin-lift is acceptable — airway takes priority over spinal precaution.
