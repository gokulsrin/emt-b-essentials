## Text

For a <b>pediatric patient</b> who will not tolerate a mask, use <b>blow-by oxygen</b>: hold the tubing or mask {{c1::2–5 cm from the face::distance}} to deliver supplemental O₂ without distressing the child.

## Extra

Blow-by delivers lower FiO₂ than a fitted mask but is better than no oxygen in a frightened child. Minimize agitation — an upset child increases O₂ demand.
