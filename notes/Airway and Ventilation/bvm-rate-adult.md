## Text

<b>BVM ventilation rate</b> for an <b>adult</b> with a pulse (not in CPR): 1 breath every {{c1::5–6 seconds::interval}}, approximately {{c2::10–12 breaths/min::rate}}.

## Extra

During CPR with an advanced airway in place, the rate becomes 1 breath every 6 seconds (10/min) asynchronous with continuous compressions. Without an advanced airway in CPR, use 30:2 compression-to-ventilation ratio.
