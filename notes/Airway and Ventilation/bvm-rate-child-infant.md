## Text

<b>BVM ventilation rate</b> for a <b>child or infant</b> with a pulse (not in CPR): 1 breath every {{c1::2–3 seconds::interval}}, approximately {{c2::20–30 breaths/min::rate}}.

## Extra

In two-rescuer pediatric/infant CPR without an advanced airway, use a 15:2 compression-to-ventilation ratio.
