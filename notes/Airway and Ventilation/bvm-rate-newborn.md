## Text

<b>BVM ventilation rate</b> for a <b>newborn</b> with a pulse: 1 breath every {{c1::1–1.5 seconds::interval}}, approximately {{c2::40–60 breaths/min::rate}}.

## Extra

Squeeze just enough to see visible chest rise over approximately 1 second — over-bagging causes gastric distension and lowers cardiac output. Always attach BVM to 15 L/min O₂ with reservoir for ~100% FiO₂.
