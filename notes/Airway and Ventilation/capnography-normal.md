## Text

<b>End-tidal CO₂ (EtCO₂)</b> normal range: {{c1::35–45 mmHg::range}}; confirms <b>tube placement</b> and monitors <b>ventilation adequacy</b>.

## Extra

Capnography measures CO₂ in exhaled breath. A waveform with a reading in the normal range is the gold standard for confirming endotracheal tube placement and ventilation.
