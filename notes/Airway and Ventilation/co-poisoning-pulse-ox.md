## Text

<b>Pulse oximetry</b> is falsely {{c1::high::direction}} in <b>carbon monoxide poisoning</b> because {{c2::CO binds hemoglobin at the same site as O₂ and the meter cannot distinguish them::reason}}.

## Extra

Treat CO poisoning clinically: high-flow O₂ via NRB regardless of pulse-ox reading. Pulse-ox also fails in shock (poor perfusion to fingertips), with nail polish, and in cold extremities.
