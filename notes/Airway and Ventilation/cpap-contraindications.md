## Text

<b>CPAP</b> is contraindicated in patients with {{c1::hypotension / shock}}, {{c1::altered mental status / inability to protect airway}}, or {{c1::apnea / no spontaneous breathing}}.

## Extra

Active vomiting is also a contraindication — aspiration risk is high with positive pressure. A patient not breathing spontaneously requires BVM ventilation, not CPAP.
