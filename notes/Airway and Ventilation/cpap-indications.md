## Text

<b>CPAP</b> is indicated for conscious patients in severe respiratory distress from {{c1::CHF / pulmonary edema}}, {{c1::severe asthma}}, or {{c1::COPD exacerbation}}.

## Extra

CPAP delivers continuous positive pressure to keep alveoli open and reduce the work of breathing. The patient must be awake enough to maintain their own airway and follow commands.
