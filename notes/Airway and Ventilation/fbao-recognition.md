## Text

<b>FBAO</b> is recognized by the <u>universal choking sign</u>; <b>mild obstruction</b>: patient can {{c1::cough forcefully and speak::ability}}; <b>severe obstruction</b>: patient has {{c2::silent cough, cannot speak, or cannot breathe::signs}}.

## Extra

With mild obstruction, encourage coughing and do not intervene beyond monitoring. Severe obstruction requires immediate action — the patient cannot clear the airway without help.
