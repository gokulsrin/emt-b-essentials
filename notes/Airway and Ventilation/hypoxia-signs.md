## Text

<b>Early hypoxia</b> signs: {{c1::restlessness}}, {{c1::anxiety}}, {{c1::tachypnea}}, {{c1::tachycardia}}.<br>
<b>Late hypoxia</b> signs: {{c2::altered LOC}}, {{c2::cyanosis}}, {{c2::bradycardia}}, {{c2::bradypnea}} — indicates pre-arrest state.

## Extra

Progression from early to late hypoxia can be rapid. Altered mental status and cyanosis are red flags to escalate intervention immediately.
