## Text

<u>Diminished or absent</u> <b>breath sounds</b> suggest {{c1::pneumothorax}}, {{c1::severe asthma ("quiet chest")}}, or {{c1::atelectasis}}.

## Extra

A "quiet chest" in an asthma patient is ominous — it means air movement is so minimal that no wheeze is audible. Treat as impending respiratory failure.
