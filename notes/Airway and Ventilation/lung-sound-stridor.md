## Text

<u>Stridor</u> — high-pitched {{c1::inspiratory::phase}} whistle indicating {{c2::upper airway obstruction::location and type}} (epiglottitis, croup, anaphylaxis, foreign body, burn).

## Extra

Stridor at rest in a child = airway emergency. Activate ALS, sit the patient upright, do not agitate, do not look in the throat (risk of complete obstruction in epiglottitis).
