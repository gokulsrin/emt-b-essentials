## Text

<b>Nasal cannula</b> maximum flow rate is {{c1::6 L/min::flow rate}}; exceeding this does not meaningfully increase FiO₂ and causes mucosal discomfort.

## Extra

At 6 L/min, the nasal cannula delivers approximately 44% FiO₂. For higher O₂ needs, switch to a non-rebreather mask.
