## Text

<b>NPA</b> is contraindicated with suspected {{c1::basilar skull fracture::injury type}} or severe {{c1::head or facial trauma::injury type}}.

## Extra

Signs of basilar skull fracture: raccoon eyes, Battle's sign, CSF leaking from nose or ears. In these cases, the NPA could enter the cranial vault.
