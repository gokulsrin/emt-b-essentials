## Text

The <b>NPA (nasopharyngeal airway)</b> is indicated when a patient needs an airway adjunct but {{c1::cannot tolerate an OPA due to intact gag reflex, or has trismus / clenched teeth}}.

## Extra

NPAs are better tolerated than OPAs in semi-conscious patients because they do not trigger the gag reflex.
