## Text

<b>NPA</b> sizing: length is measured from the {{c1::tip of the nose to the earlobe::landmark pair}}; diameter should approximate the patient's {{c2::smallest finger::anatomic reference}}.

## Extra

If the NPA is too long it can enter the esophagus; if too short it will not maintain the airway past the soft palate.
