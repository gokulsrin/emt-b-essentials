## Text

Before placing a <b>non-rebreather mask</b>, {{c1::fill the reservoir bag fully::step}} by occluding the one-way valve while flowing O₂ at {{c2::15 L/min::flow rate}}.

## Extra

A partially inflated reservoir delivers less than the intended FiO₂. The bag should never fully collapse during inhalation — if it does, increase the flow rate.
