## Text

<b>OPA</b> is contraindicated in patients with {{c1::an intact gag reflex}}, {{c1::responsiveness}}, or {{c1::vomitus / active bleeding in the airway}}.

## Extra

If any contraindication is present, consider an NPA — it is better tolerated in semi-conscious patients.
