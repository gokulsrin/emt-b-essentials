## Text

<b>OPA insertion (adult):</b> insert with the tip pointed toward the {{c1::roof of the mouth}}, then rotate {{c2::180°}} as you advance past the tongue.

## Extra

In pediatric patients, insert right-side-up using a tongue depressor — do NOT rotate, to avoid trauma to the soft palate.
