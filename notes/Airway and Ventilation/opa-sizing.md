## Text

<b>OPA</b> sizing: measure from the {{c1::corner of the mouth to the angle of the jaw (or earlobe)::landmark pair}}.

## Extra

An incorrectly sized OPA can occlude the airway: too long pushes the epiglottis over the glottis; too short pushes the tongue back.
