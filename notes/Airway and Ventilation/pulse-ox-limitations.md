## Text

<b>Pulse oximetry</b> is falsely elevated in {{c1::carbon monoxide poisoning}} and {{c1::methemoglobinemia}} because the sensor cannot distinguish abnormal hemoglobin from oxyhemoglobin.

## Extra

Additional causes of inaccurate pulse-ox: nail polish, cold extremities, and poor peripheral perfusion (shock). Always treat the patient clinically, not just the number.
