## Text

<b>Pulse oximetry</b> is unreliable or unreadable with {{c1::nail polish or dark pigment}}, {{c1::cold extremities}}, and {{c1::hypoperfusion / shock}}.

## Extra

These conditions reduce the signal at the sensor site. Move the probe to a warmer location (ear, forehead) or warm the extremity when peripheral readings are poor.
