## Text

<b>Recovery position</b>: place an unconscious patient who is breathing and has no suspected spinal trauma in the {{c1::left lateral recumbent::position}} position.

## Extra

The lateral position allows secretions and vomit to drain by gravity, reducing aspiration risk. Contraindicated if spinal injury is suspected — maintain inline stabilization instead.
