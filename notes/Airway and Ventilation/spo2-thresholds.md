## Text

<b>SpO₂ thresholds:</b> normal on room air is {{c1::95–100%::range}}; begin supplemental O₂ when SpO₂ drops below {{c2::94%::threshold}} (or when symptomatic regardless of reading); COPD / chronic hypoxia target is {{c3::88–92%::range}}.

## Extra

In COPD, targeting a lower SpO₂ range avoids blunting the hypoxic drive on which some chronic CO₂ retainers rely.
