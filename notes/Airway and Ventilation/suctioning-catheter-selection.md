## Text

For <b>oropharyngeal suctioning</b>, use a {{c1::rigid (Yankauer / tonsil-tip)::catheter type}} catheter; use a soft French catheter for {{c2::nasal or ETT suctioning::location}}.

## Extra

Never suction blindly past what you can see — measure depth the same way you size an OPA to avoid stimulating the vagus nerve deep in the airway.
