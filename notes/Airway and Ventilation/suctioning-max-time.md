## Text

Maximum <b>suction time per pass</b>: {{c1::15 seconds::duration}} in adults, {{c1::10 seconds::duration}} in children, {{c1::5 seconds::duration}} in infants.

## Extra

Prolonged suctioning causes hypoxia, bradycardia (vagal), and mucosal trauma. Count out loud or use a watch to stay within limits.
