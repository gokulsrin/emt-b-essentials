## Text

During <b>suctioning</b>, insert the catheter with suction {{c1::off::on or off?}}, then apply suction only while {{c2::withdrawing::direction}}.

## Extra

This technique prevents mucosal trauma during insertion and maximizes suction effectiveness during removal.
