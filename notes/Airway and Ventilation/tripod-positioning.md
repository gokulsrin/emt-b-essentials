## Text

<u>Tripod positioning</u> — leaning forward with hands on knees — combined with <b>retractions</b> and <b>accessory muscle use</b> signals {{c1::severe respiratory distress::severity}}.

## Extra

Retractions (supraclavicular, intercostal, substernal) indicate the patient is generating extreme negative pressure to breathe. Tripod maximizes diaphragm excursion and upper airway diameter.
