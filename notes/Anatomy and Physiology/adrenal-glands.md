## Text

<b>Adrenal cortex</b> secretes {{c1::cortisol and aldosterone::hormones}}; <b>adrenal medulla</b> secretes {{c2::epinephrine and norepinephrine::hormones}}.

## Extra

Cortisol: stress response, anti-inflammatory, blood sugar regulation. Aldosterone: sodium and water retention, blood pressure. The medulla is the sympathetic relay — its hormones drive the fight-or-flight response.
