## Text

The four major <b>body cavities</b>:
{{c1::thoracic}}, {{c1::abdominal}}, {{c1::pelvic}}, {{c1::retroperitoneal}}.

## Extra

Thoracic: heart, lungs, great vessels. Abdominal: stomach, intestines, liver, spleen. Pelvic: bladder, reproductive organs, rectum. Retroperitoneal (behind the peritoneum): kidneys, aorta, inferior vena cava, pancreas.
