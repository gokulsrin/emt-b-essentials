## Text

<b>Cardiac conduction pathway</b>, in order:
{{c1::SA node}} → {{c1::AV node}} → {{c1::Bundle of His}} → {{c1::right & left bundle branches}} → {{c1::Purkinje fibers}} → ventricular contraction.

## Extra

The SA node is the natural pacemaker (~60–100 bpm). The AV node delays the impulse to allow the atria to empty before the ventricles contract. If the SA node fails, the AV node takes over (~40–60 bpm); if both fail, ventricular escape pacemakers fire at ~20–40 bpm — slow, unstable, and a sign of critical emergency. A new bradycardia with hypotension warrants immediate action.
