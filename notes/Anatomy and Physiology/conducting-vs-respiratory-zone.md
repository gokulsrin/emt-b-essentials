## Text

The <b>conducting zone</b> (nose to terminal bronchioles) moves air but has {{c1::no gas exchange::function}}; gas exchange begins at the {{c2::respiratory bronchioles and alveoli::structures}}.

## Extra

Dead space is the volume in the conducting zone that never participates in gas exchange (~150 mL in adults). Diseases that increase dead space (pulmonary embolism) raise CO2 and lower effective ventilation even with normal breathing effort.
