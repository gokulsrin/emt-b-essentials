## Text

Primary muscle of <b>respiration</b>: {{c1::diaphragm::muscle}}. Accessory muscles recruited during respiratory distress: {{c2::intercostals, sternocleidomastoid (SCM), and scalenes::muscles}}.

## Extra

Diaphragm contraction flattens it, increasing thoracic volume and drawing air in. Accessory muscle use (visible neck muscle straining, intercostal retractions) signals significant respiratory distress — a critical finding on scene.
