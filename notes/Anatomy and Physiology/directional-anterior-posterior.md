## Text

<b>Anterior</b> (ventral) means {{c1::toward the front::direction}}; <b>posterior</b> (dorsal) means {{c2::toward the back::direction}}.

## Extra

Anterior chest wall = the front of the chest. Posterior tibial pulse = behind the inner ankle. Mid-clavicular line and mid-axillary line are commonly used anterior/lateral reference lines for documenting trauma findings.
