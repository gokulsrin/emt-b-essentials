## Text

<b>Medial</b> means {{c1::toward the midline::direction}}; <b>lateral</b> means {{c2::away from the midline::direction}}.

## Extra

The nose is medial to the eyes; the ears are lateral to the nose. Right and left always refer to the patient's right and left, not the provider's.
