## Text

<b>Proximal</b> means {{c1::closer to the trunk / point of origin::direction}}; <b>distal</b> means {{c2::farther from the trunk / point of origin::direction}}.

## Extra

The elbow is proximal to the wrist; the wrist is distal to the elbow. In bleeding control, "proximal" to a wound means between the wound and the heart — important for tourniquet placement.
