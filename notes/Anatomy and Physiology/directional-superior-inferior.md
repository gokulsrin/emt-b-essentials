## Text

<b>Superior</b> means {{c1::toward the head::direction}}; <b>inferior</b> means {{c2::toward the feet::direction}}.

## Extra

The heart is superior to the diaphragm; the liver is inferior to the diaphragm. In documentation, "superior border of the wound" means the edge closest to the patient's head.
