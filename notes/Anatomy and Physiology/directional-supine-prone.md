## Text

<b>Supine</b> means {{c1::lying face-up::position}}; <b>prone</b> means {{c2::lying face-down::position}}.

## Extra

Unresponsive patients found prone should be log-rolled supine for assessment and CPR. Recovery position is a lateral decubitus position (on the side), used for unconscious but breathing patients to protect the airway.
