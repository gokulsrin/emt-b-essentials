## Text

<b>Insulin</b> {{c1::lowers::direction}} blood glucose; <b>glucagon</b> {{c2::raises::direction}} blood glucose.

## Extra

Insulin is secreted by beta cells of the pancreatic islets in response to high glucose. Glucagon is secreted by alpha cells in response to low glucose and stimulates hepatic glycogen breakdown. Diabetic emergencies result from imbalance of these two hormones.
