## Text

The <b>gallbladder</b> {{c1::stores and concentrates bile::function}}, then releases it into the duodenum to aid {{c2::fat digestion::process}}.

## Extra

Bile is produced by the liver and stored in the gallbladder until a fatty meal stimulates release via the common bile duct. Gallstones blocking the duct cause RUQ pain, nausea, and vomiting — classic biliary colic.
