## Text

The <b>genitourinary tract</b> in order:
{{c1::kidneys}} → {{c1::ureters}} → {{c1::bladder}} → {{c1::urethra}}.

## Extra

Each kidney connects to the bladder via a ureter. Kidney stones typically cause excruciating flank pain radiating to the groin as the stone moves down the ureter. The urethra is shorter in females, increasing susceptibility to UTIs.
