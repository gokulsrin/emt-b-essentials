## Text

Five <b>synovial joint types</b>:
{{c1::ball-and-socket}} (hip, shoulder), {{c1::hinge}} (elbow, knee), {{c1::pivot}} (C1–C2 rotation), {{c1::gliding}} (wrist carpals), {{c1::saddle}} (thumb CMC).

## Extra

Ball-and-socket joints allow the greatest range of motion (flexion, extension, rotation, abduction, adduction). Hinge joints allow only flexion and extension. Knowing joint type helps anticipate dislocation mechanism and reduction technique.
