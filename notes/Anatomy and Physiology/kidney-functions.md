## Text

The <b>kidneys</b> filter blood and regulate {{c1::fluid and electrolyte balance::function}}, {{c1::blood pressure::function}} (via renin), and produce {{c1::erythropoietin (EPO)::hormone}} to stimulate red blood cell production.

## Extra

Chronic kidney disease causes hypertension, electrolyte imbalances, and anemia (from low EPO). Dialysis patients are at risk for hyperkalemia between sessions — a potentially fatal arrhythmia trigger.
