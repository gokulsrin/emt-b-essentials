## Text

Four key functions of the <b>liver</b>:
{{c1::detoxification}} of drugs/alcohol, {{c1::bile production}} for fat digestion, {{c1::glycogen storage}} for glucose regulation, {{c1::clotting factor synthesis}}.

## Extra

Liver failure impairs all four — expect drug accumulation, fat malabsorption (steatorrhea), hypoglycemia, and coagulopathy. The liver is highly vascular; blunt abdominal trauma can cause life-threatening hemorrhage even without penetrating injury.
