## Text

Parts of a <b>long bone</b>:
{{c1::epiphysis}} (rounded ends), {{c1::diaphysis}} (shaft), {{c1::metaphysis}} (growth plate region), {{c1::periosteum}} (outer membrane), {{c1::medullary cavity}} (inner marrow space).

## Extra

The periosteum is richly innervated — periosteal pain is why fractures hurt intensely. The medullary cavity contains red marrow (blood cell production) in flat bones and yellow marrow (fat) in adult long bones.
