## Text

The <b>pancreas</b> has two roles: <b>endocrine</b> — secretes {{c1::insulin and glucagon::hormones}} into the blood; <b>exocrine</b> — secretes {{c2::digestive enzymes::product}} into the duodenum.

## Extra

Insulin lowers blood glucose; glucagon raises it. Pancreatitis occurs when exocrine enzymes activate inside the pancreas and autodigest it — presenting with severe epigastric pain radiating to the back.
