## Text

From the main bronchi, air travels through {{c1::secondary and tertiary bronchi::structures}},
then into {{c1::bronchioles::structures}}, and finally reaches the
{{c1::alveoli::structures}} where gas exchange occurs across the
<b>alveolar-capillary membrane</b>.

## Extra

Gas exchange is driven by diffusion: O2 crosses from the alveoli (high concentration) into capillary blood, while CO2 crosses in the opposite direction. Diseases like pulmonary edema thicken this membrane and impair exchange.
