## Text

Below the larynx, air passes through the {{c1::trachea::structure}}, which splits into the
{{c1::right and left main bronchi::structures}}. The <b>right mainstem bronchus</b> is
{{c2::wider and more vertical::characteristic}} than the left.

## Extra

Because the right mainstem bronchus is wider and more vertical, aspirated foreign bodies and misplaced ET tubes preferentially enter the right side. Confirm ET tube placement bilaterally after intubation.
