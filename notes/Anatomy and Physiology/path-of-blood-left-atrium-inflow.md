## Text

The <b>left atrium</b> receives oxygenated blood returning from the lungs via the
{{c1::pulmonary veins::vessels}}.

## Extra

The pulmonary veins carry oxygenated blood — they are the only veins in the body that do so. Four pulmonary veins drain into the left atrium (2 from each lung).
