## Text

The pulmonary <u>arteries</u> are the only arteries that carry
{{c1::deoxygenated::oxygenation status}} blood; the pulmonary <u>veins</u> are the
only veins that carry {{c2::oxygenated::oxygenation status}} blood.

## Extra

This naming paradox trips up many learners. Arteries are defined by direction of flow (away from the heart), not oxygen content.
