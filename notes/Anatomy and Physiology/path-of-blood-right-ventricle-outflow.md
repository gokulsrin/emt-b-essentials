## Text

Blood leaves the <b>right ventricle</b> through the {{c1::pulmonic valve::valve}}
into the {{c1::pulmonary arteries::vessels}}, headed for the lungs.

## Extra

The pulmonary arteries carry deoxygenated blood — they are the only arteries in the body that do so.
