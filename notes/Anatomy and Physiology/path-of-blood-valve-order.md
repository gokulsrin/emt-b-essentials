## Text

Heart valve order from <b>right atrium</b> outward (mnemonic <b>TPMA</b>):
{{c1::tricuspid}}, {{c1::pulmonic}}, {{c1::mitral}}, {{c1::aortic}}.

## Extra

AV valves (tricuspid, mitral) separate atria from ventricles; semilunar valves (pulmonic, aortic) separate ventricles from outflow vessels. Mitral is also called the bicuspid valve.
