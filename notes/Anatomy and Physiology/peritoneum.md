## Text

<b>Visceral peritoneum</b> covers the {{c1::abdominal organs::location}}; <b>parietal peritoneum</b> lines the {{c2::abdominal wall::location}}. Inflammation of the peritoneum is called {{c3::peritonitis::condition}}.

## Extra

Peritonitis presents with board-like rigidity, guarding, and rebound tenderness — classic signs of an acute abdomen. Causes include ruptured appendix, perforated ulcer, and bowel perforation. Any suspected peritonitis is a surgical emergency.
