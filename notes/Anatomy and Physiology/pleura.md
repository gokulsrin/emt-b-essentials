## Text

<b>Visceral pleura</b> covers the {{c1::lung surface::location}}; <b>parietal pleura</b> lines the {{c2::chest wall::location}}. The intrapleural space maintains {{c3::negative pressure::pressure type}}, keeping the lungs expanded.

## Extra

When the intrapleural space fills with air (pneumothorax), negative pressure is lost and the lung collapses. Tension pneumothorax traps air and shifts the mediastinum — life-threatening without needle decompression.
