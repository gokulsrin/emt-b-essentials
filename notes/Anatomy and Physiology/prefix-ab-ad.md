## Text

<b>Ab-</b> means {{c1::away from::meaning}}; <b>ad-</b> means {{c2::toward::meaning}}.

## Extra

Abduction = movement away from the midline; adduction = movement toward the midline. Remembered by: "Add" = toward (adding to the body). These appear in orthopedic and neurological assessments.
