## Text

<b>Dys-</b> means {{c1::difficult / painful::meaning}}; <b>a-</b> / <b>an-</b> means {{c2::without / absence of::meaning}}.

## Extra

Dyspnea = difficulty breathing; apnea = absence of breathing. Dysrhythmia = abnormal rhythm; asystole = no electrical activity. Dys- implies something is happening but poorly; a-/an- implies complete absence.
