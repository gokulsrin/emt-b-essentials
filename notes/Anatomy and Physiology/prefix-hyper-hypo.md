## Text

<b>Hyper-</b> means {{c1::high / above normal::meaning}}; <b>hypo-</b> means {{c2::low / below normal::meaning}}.

## Extra

Hypertension = BP above normal; hypotension = BP below normal. Hyperglycemia vs hypoglycemia. These two prefixes appear constantly in patient reports and lab values.
