## Text

<b>Peri-</b> means {{c1::around::meaning}}; <b>inter-</b> means {{c1::between::meaning}}; <b>intra-</b> means {{c1::within::meaning}}.

## Extra

Pericardium = sac around the heart. Intercostal = between the ribs. Intravenous = within the vein. These three are commonly confused — peri vs intra is especially important in procedures (pericardiocentesis vs intracardiac).
