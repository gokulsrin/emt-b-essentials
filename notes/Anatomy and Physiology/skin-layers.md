## Text

The three layers of <b>skin</b>, superficial to deep: {{c1::epidermis}}, {{c1::dermis}}, {{c1::subcutaneous layer (hypodermis)}}.

## Extra

Epidermis: outermost, no blood vessels. Dermis: capillaries, nerve endings, sweat glands. Subcutaneous: fat and larger vessels. Burn depth maps to layers — superficial (epidermis), partial-thickness (into dermis, painful, blistering), full-thickness (through dermis; less pain because nerve endings are destroyed).
