## Text

The three layers of <b>skin</b>, superficial to deep: {{c1::epidermis}}, {{c1::dermis}}, {{c1::subcutaneous layer (hypodermis)}}.

## Extra

Epidermis: outermost, mostly dead keratinocytes, no blood vessels. Dermis: capillaries, sweat glands, hair follicles, nerve endings (pain and temperature). Subcutaneous: fat, larger vessels and nerves, insulation. Burn depth maps to these layers — superficial (epidermis), partial-thickness (into dermis, blistering, very painful), full-thickness (through to subcutaneous; less pain because nerve endings are destroyed).
