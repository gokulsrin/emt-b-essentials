## Text

<b>Spinal column</b> vertebra counts, top to bottom:
{{c1::7}} cervical (C), {{c1::12}} thoracic (T), {{c1::5}} lumbar (L), {{c1::5}} sacral (S), {{c1::4}} coccygeal (Co).

## Extra

Mnemonic for meals: "Breakfast at 7, Lunch at 12, Dinner at 5." The cervical region (C3–C5) controls the diaphragm — injuries at or above C3 cause respiratory arrest. Lumbar injuries commonly cause paraplegia.
