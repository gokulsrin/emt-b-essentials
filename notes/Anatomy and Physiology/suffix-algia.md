## Text

The suffix <b>-algia</b> means {{c1::pain::meaning}}.

## Extra

Myalgia = muscle pain; neuralgia = nerve pain; arthralgia = joint pain. On a call, a patient describing "myalgia" is reporting muscle aches — common with flu-like illness or exertion.
