## Text

The suffix <b>-emesis</b> means {{c1::vomiting::meaning}}.

## Extra

Hematemesis = vomiting blood (from the GI tract — coffee-ground or bright red). Distinguish from hemoptysis, which is coughed-up blood from the airway (usually frothy, bright red).
