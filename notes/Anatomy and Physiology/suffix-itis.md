## Text

The suffix <b>-itis</b> means {{c1::inflammation::meaning}}.

## Extra

Appendicitis = inflamed appendix; pancreatitis = inflamed pancreas; otitis = inflamed ear. Inflammation typically presents with redness, warmth, swelling, and pain at the affected site.
