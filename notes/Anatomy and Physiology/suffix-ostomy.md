## Text

The suffix <b>-ostomy</b> means {{c1::surgically created opening::meaning}}.

## Extra

Colostomy = opening from colon to abdominal wall. Tracheostomy = surgical opening in the trachea. Patients with ostomies may have collection bags or stomas visible during your assessment.
