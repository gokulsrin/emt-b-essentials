## Text

The suffix <b>-plegia</b> means {{c1::paralysis::meaning}}.

## Extra

Hemiplegia = one-sided paralysis (common after stroke). Quadriplegia = paralysis of all four limbs (spinal cord injury at C5 or above). Paraplegia = paralysis of both legs (thoracic/lumbar spinal injury).
