## Front

Compare the <b>sympathetic</b> ("fight or flight") and <b>parasympathetic</b> ("rest and digest") branches of the autonomic nervous system.

## Back

| Effect | Sympathetic | Parasympathetic |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Heart rate | ↑ | ↓ |
| Blood pressure | ↑ (vasoconstriction) | ↓ |
| Bronchioles | dilate | constrict |
| Pupils | dilate (mydriasis) | constrict (miosis) |
| GI motility | ↓ | ↑ |
| Sweating | ↑ | minimal |
| Main neurotransmitter | norepinephrine / epinephrine | acetylcholine |

## Extra

The sympathetic response is the shock picture: pale, cool, clammy skin (vasoconstriction plus sweating) with tachycardia. Parasympathetic overdrive — from carotid pressure, vagal maneuvers, or organophosphate poisoning (SLUDGE mnemonic) — produces the opposite: bradycardia, bronchospasm, increased secretions.
