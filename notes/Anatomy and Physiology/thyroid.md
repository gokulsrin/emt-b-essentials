## Text

The <b>thyroid gland</b> regulates {{c1::metabolism::function}} by secreting {{c2::T3 and T4::hormones}}.

## Extra

Hyperthyroidism (too much T3/T4): tachycardia, heat intolerance, weight loss, anxiety. Hypothyroidism (too little): bradycardia, cold intolerance, weight gain, fatigue. Thyroid storm is a life-threatening hypermetabolic crisis.
