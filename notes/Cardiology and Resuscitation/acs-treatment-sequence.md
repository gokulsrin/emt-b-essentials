## Text

<b>EMT field care sequence</b> for suspected <b>ACS</b>:

1. {{c1::Position of comfort}} (usually semi-Fowler's); calm patient, limit exertion
2. {{c1::Oxygen only if SpO₂ < 94%}} — routine high-flow O₂ in normoxic ACS not recommended
3. {{c1::Aspirin 324 mg chewed}} if no contraindication
4. {{c1::Nitroglycerin 0.4 mg SL}} if SBP > 100 and no PDE-5 inhibitor use and no right-sided MI
5. {{c1::12-lead ECG}} if available; rapid transport to <u>PCI-capable hospital</u>
6. {{c1::Reassess vitals, pain, mental status every 5 minutes}}

## Extra

The old <b>MONA</b> mnemonic is outdated as a sequence — modern care prioritizes ASA early, O₂ only if hypoxic, nitro per protocol. Nitro contraindications: SBP under 100, PDE-5 inhibitor use, suspected right-sided MI.
