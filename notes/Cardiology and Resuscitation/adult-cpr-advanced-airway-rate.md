## Text

With an <b>advanced airway</b> in place during adult CPR: give continuous compressions and ventilate every {{c1::6 seconds (10 breaths/min)::rate}}.

## Extra

No longer pause for ventilations once an advanced airway (supraglottic or ETT) secures the airway.
Asynchronous compressions maximize chest compression fraction. Avoid hyperventilation.
