## Text

Adult <b>CPR</b>: maximum interruption duration for any pulse check or rhythm analysis is {{c1::10 seconds::time limit}}.

## Extra

Every second without compressions reduces coronary perfusion pressure. The pre-shock pause
should also be minimized to under 10 seconds. Resume compressions immediately after shock delivery.
