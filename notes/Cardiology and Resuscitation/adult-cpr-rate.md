## Text

Adult <b>high-quality CPR</b> compression rate: {{c1::100–120::compressions/min}}.

## Extra

Per 2020 AHA guidelines. A rate above 120/min degrades compression quality
(shallower, less recoil time). A rate below 100/min is insufficient perfusion pressure.
