## Text

Between each adult <b>CPR</b> compression, allow {{c1::full chest recoil::recoil requirement}}; do not lean on the chest.

## Extra

Leaning on the chest between compressions prevents full recoil, raising intrathoracic pressure
and reducing venous return to the right heart — decreasing coronary and cerebral perfusion.
