## Text

<b>AED</b> — <u>excessive chest hair</u>: {{c1::apply the first pad set, rip off to remove hair, then apply a fresh set::action}}.

## Extra

Chest hair prevents adequate pad-to-skin contact, increasing impedance and reducing shock efficacy.
If a razor is immediately available, shave the pad sites; if not, the "rip and replace" method is faster
and acceptable per AHA guidelines.
