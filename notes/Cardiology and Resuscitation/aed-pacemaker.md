## Text

<b>AED</b> — implanted pacemaker or ICD (palpable lump under skin): place AED pad at least {{c1::1 inch (2.5 cm)::distance}} away from the device.

## Extra

Placing a pad directly over a pacemaker or ICD can damage the device, reduce shock efficacy,
or cause arcing. The subclavicular area on the right is the most common implant site —
shift the right pad slightly if necessary.
