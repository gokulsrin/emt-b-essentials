## Text

<b>AED</b> — child under 8 years or under 25 kg: use {{c1::pediatric pads / dose attenuator::first choice}} if available; if unavailable, {{c2::adult pads::alternative}} are acceptable (use <u>anterior-posterior placement</u> to prevent pad contact).

## Extra

Pediatric pads reduce delivered energy to a child-appropriate dose (roughly 50–75 J vs.
150–200 J for adults). If only adult pads are available, do not delay defibrillation —
use them. Anterior-posterior placement (one pad on the chest, one on the back) prevents
the pads from touching each other on a small chest.
