## Text

<b>AED</b> — pregnancy: use the AED with {{c1::standard pad placement, no modification::modification needed}}.

## Extra

The best intervention for the fetus is successful maternal resuscitation. Additionally,
perform manual left uterine displacement (push the uterus to the patient's left side)
to relieve aortocaval compression while continuing compressions and defibrillation.
