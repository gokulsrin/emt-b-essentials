## Text

<b>AED</b> — transdermal medication patch (e.g., nitroglycerin, fentanyl): {{c1::remove the patch and wipe the skin::action}} before applying AED pads.

## Extra

Metal foil backing in the patch can cause arcing and burns under the pads. The medication
coating can also absorb current. Use gloves when removing — the drug is still active.
