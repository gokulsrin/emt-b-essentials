## Text

<b>AED 2-person operation</b>: one rescuer performs {{c1::continuous chest compressions::role 1}} while the other {{c1::applies pads and operates the AED::role 2}}; pause compressions only for {{c1::rhythm analysis and shock delivery::when to pause}}.

## Extra

Minimize hands-off time — the compressor keeps going while the AED is set up. Resume compressions immediately after shock; switch compressors every 2 minutes to prevent fatigue.
