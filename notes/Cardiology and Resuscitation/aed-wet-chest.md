## Text

<b>AED</b> — <u>wet chest</u>: {{c1::quickly dry the chest::action}} before applying pads; also move patient out of standing water.

## Extra

Water on the skin creates a conductive path that can divert current away from the heart
and toward the rescuer. Move the patient out of standing water if possible; dry with a towel.
