## Text

AHA <b>Out-of-Hospital Cardiac Arrest Chain of Survival</b> (6 links):
{{c1::recognition and activation of emergency response}}, {{c1::early high-quality CPR}}, {{c1::rapid defibrillation}}, {{c1::advanced resuscitation}}, {{c1::post-arrest care}}, {{c1::recovery}}.

## Extra

Each link is independent but all are required for best outcome. "Recovery" was added in 2020 to address long-term rehabilitation needs of cardiac arrest survivors.
