## Text

<b>Conscious adult choking</b> (severe obstruction — cannot speak, cough, or breathe): perform {{c1::abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver)::intervention}} repeated until the object is expelled or the patient becomes unresponsive.

## Extra

AHA also accepts 5 back blows between each set of abdominal thrusts as an alternative. Stand behind the patient, fist just above the navel and well below the xiphoid, deliver firm inward-upward thrusts.
