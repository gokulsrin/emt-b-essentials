## Text

<b>Conscious child choking</b> (age 1 year to puberty, severe obstruction): perform {{c1::abdominal thrusts::intervention}} with {{c1::lower force than in adults::force adjustment}}.

## Extra

Technique mirrors adult Heimlich but scaled to the child's size — kneel or crouch behind the child and use gentler inward-upward thrusts. Continue until obstruction clears or child loses consciousness; if unresponsive, start CPR.
