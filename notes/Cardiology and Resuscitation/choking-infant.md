## Text

<b>Infant choking</b> (under 1 year, severe obstruction): alternate {{c1::5 back blows::first step}} then {{c1::5 chest thrusts::second step}}; <u>do not use abdominal thrusts</u>.

## Extra

Support the infant face-down on your forearm for back blows (heel of hand between shoulder blades), then flip face-up for chest thrusts at the same landmark as infant CPR. Repeat the cycle until the object is expelled or the infant becomes unresponsive.
