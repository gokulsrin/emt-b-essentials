## Text

<b>Choking patient who becomes unresponsive</b>: {{c1::start CPR::immediate action}}; before each rescue breath, {{c1::look in the mouth::check}} and remove only {{c1::visible objects::what to remove}}.

## Extra

Do not perform blind finger sweeps — they can push the object deeper. Only remove objects you can directly see in the mouth. Continue CPR until the obstruction clears or advanced care arrives.
