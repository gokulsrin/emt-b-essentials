## Text

<b>DNR order</b> in the field: {{c1::honor it if the order is valid::action}}; when in doubt, {{c1::begin resuscitation::default action}}.

## Extra

A valid DNR must meet local/state requirements (signed, dated, patient-specific). If the order is absent, unclear, or you cannot verify it, err toward starting resuscitation — you can always stop once validity is confirmed. Medical direction governs final decisions.
