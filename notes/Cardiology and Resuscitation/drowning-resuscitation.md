## Text

<b>Drowning</b> resuscitation: begin with {{c1::5 rescue breaths::initial step}} before starting chest compressions, because drowning arrest is {{c1::respiratory::arrest type}} in origin.

## Extra

Most drowning victims are hypoxic before cardiac arrest occurs. The 5 initial rescue breaths address the primary problem. After the initial breaths, follow standard CPR sequence and apply AED as soon as available.
