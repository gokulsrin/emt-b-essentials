## Text

<b>Obvious irreversible signs of death</b> (withhold resuscitation): {{c1::rigor mortis}}, {{c1::dependent lividity}}, {{c1::decapitation}}, {{c1::decomposition}}, {{c1::incineration}}.

## Extra

These findings indicate death occurred well before EMS arrival and resuscitation cannot succeed. Local protocols and medical direction may specify additional criteria (e.g., valid DNR order).
