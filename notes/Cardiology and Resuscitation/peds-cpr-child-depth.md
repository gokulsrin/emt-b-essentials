## Text

<b>Child CPR</b> (age 1 year to puberty) compression depth: {{c1::about 2 inches::depth}} (approximately ⅓ AP chest diameter).

## Extra

"One-third the AP diameter of the chest" is the universal pediatric depth rule.
In children this works out to roughly 2 inches; in infants roughly 1.5 inches.
