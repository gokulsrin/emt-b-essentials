## Text

<b>Infant CPR</b> (under 1 year) compression depth: {{c1::about 1.5 inches::depth}} (approximately ⅓ AP chest diameter).

## Extra

Same proportional rule as children (⅓ AP diameter), but infant chest is much smaller.
Do not compress over the xiphoid process — landmark is just below the nipple line.
