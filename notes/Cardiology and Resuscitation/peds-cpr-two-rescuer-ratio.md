## Text

<b>Two-rescuer CPR</b> compression-to-ventilation ratio for <u>children and infants</u>: {{c1::15:2::ratio}} (vs. 30:2 for adult two-rescuer).

## Extra

The lower ratio (more frequent breaths relative to compressions) reflects the respiratory
origin of most pediatric arrests. Compare: adult two-rescuer ratio remains 30:2.
