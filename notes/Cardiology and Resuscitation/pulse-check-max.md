## Text

Maximum time to spend on a <b>pulse check</b> during resuscitation: {{c1::10 seconds::time limit}}.

## Extra

If a pulse is not definitely felt within 10 seconds, assume pulselessness and resume or begin CPR. This applies to all age groups.
