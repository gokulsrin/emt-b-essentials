## Text

<b>Shockable</b> cardiac arrest rhythms: {{c1::ventricular fibrillation (V-fib)}}, {{c1::pulseless ventricular tachycardia (V-tach)}}.<br>
<b>Non-shockable</b> rhythms: {{c2::asystole}}, {{c2::pulseless electrical activity (PEA)}}.

## Extra

The AED will analyze and advise shock only for shockable rhythms. Asystole and PEA require high-quality CPR and treatment of reversible causes (the Hs and Ts) — defibrillation does not help.
