## Text

<b>Abdominal aortic aneurysm (AAA)</b> classic presentation: <u>tearing or ripping back/abdominal pain</u>, {{c1::pulsatile abdominal mass}}, and {{c2::hypotension}}; do NOT palpate aggressively.

## Extra

Aggressive palpation can rupture a leaking aneurysm. Transport immediately; survival depends on OR time. Hypotension + pulsatile mass in an older patient = AAA until proven otherwise.
