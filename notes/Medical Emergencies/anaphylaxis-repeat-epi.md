## Text

In <b>anaphylaxis</b>, repeat epinephrine every {{c1::5–15 minutes::interval}} if symptoms persist (per protocol).

## Extra

Most systems carry two auto-injectors for this reason. Biphasic anaphylaxis (return of symptoms hours later) is another reason all anaphylaxis patients require transport regardless of apparent recovery.
