## Text

<b>Anaphylaxis</b> requires a severe allergic exposure PLUS at least one of: {{c1::airway/respiratory compromise}} (stridor, wheezing, hoarseness, tongue/lip swelling) OR {{c1::cardiovascular compromise}} (hypotension, syncope, weak pulses, AMS).

## Extra

Skin findings (hives, flushing, itching) and GI symptoms (vomiting, diarrhea) are supportive but NOT required to diagnose anaphylaxis. The airway or cardiovascular component is the diagnostic threshold that mandates epinephrine.
