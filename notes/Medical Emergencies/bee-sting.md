## Text

For a <b>bee/wasp sting</b>: remove the stinger by {{c1::scraping (not squeezing) it off::method}}, then monitor closely for signs of {{c2::anaphylaxis::complication}}.

## Extra

Squeezing or using tweezers injects more venom from the attached sac. A flat card edge or fingernail scraped against the skin removes the stinger efficiently. Any systemic reaction (hives, wheezing, hypotension) requires epinephrine.
