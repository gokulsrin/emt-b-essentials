## Text

When responding to a behavioral emergency, the first priority is {{c1::scene safety::priority}} — assess for weapons or agitation before entering; use a {{c2::calm, non-threatening tone::approach}} and avoid cornering the patient.

## Extra

Never position yourself between the patient and the exit — always maintain an egress route. Law enforcement should clear the scene before EMS enters if violence is possible.
