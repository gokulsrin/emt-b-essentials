## Text

In suspected stroke, always document the <b>time of last known well</b> — defined as the last time the patient was {{c1::seen at their normal baseline::definition}} — because it drives eligibility for {{c2::tPA / thrombectomy::interventions}}.

## Extra

"Last known well" is not "when symptoms were noticed" — it is when the patient was last NORMAL. This distinction is critical for the tPA eligibility window (typically 3–4.5 hours) and mechanical thrombectomy (up to 24 hours in select patients).
