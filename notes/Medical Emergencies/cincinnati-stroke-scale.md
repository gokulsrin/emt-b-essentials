## Text

<b>Cincinnati Prehospital Stroke Scale</b> — three components (named triad, cloze all):
{{c1::facial droop}}, {{c1::arm drift}}, {{c1::speech/language abnormality}}.

## Extra

Facial droop: ask patient to smile — one side doesn't move equally. Arm drift: eyes closed, arms out 10 sec — one drifts down. Speech: repeat a phrase ("you can't teach an old dog new tricks") — slurred, wrong words, or unable to speak.
