## Text

In a <b>COPD exacerbation</b>, titrate supplemental O₂ to maintain SpO₂ of {{c1::88–92%::target range}}, because excessive O₂ can suppress the {{c2::hypoxic drive::mechanism}} in chronic CO₂ retainers.

## Extra

COPD patients can become CO₂ retainers who rely on hypoxia as their primary respiratory stimulus. Targeting 88–92% avoids both hypoxia and the risk of O₂-induced respiratory depression. If the patient is in respiratory failure, BVM ventilation trumps O₂ titration concerns.
