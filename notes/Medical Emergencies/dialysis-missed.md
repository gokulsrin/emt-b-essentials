## Text

A dialysis patient who missed treatments is at risk for <b>hyperkalemia</b>, presenting as {{c1::peaked T waves on ECG, weakness, and dysrhythmia::findings}}; treat with high-flow O₂ and {{c2::rapid transport (ALS)::disposition}}.

## Extra

Hyperkalemia can cause fatal ventricular dysrhythmias. EMTs cannot give calcium or sodium bicarbonate (ALS drugs), but early ALS rendezvous is critical. Also anticipate fluid overload and pulmonary edema from missed ultrafiltration.
