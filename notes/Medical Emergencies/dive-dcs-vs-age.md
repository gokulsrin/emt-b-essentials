## Text

Dive emergencies: <b>DCS</b> (decompression sickness) → {{c1::joint pain ("the bends"), marbling, fatigue::presentation}}; <b>AGE</b> (arterial gas embolism) → {{c2::sudden focal neurologic deficit immediately on surfacing::presentation}}.

## Extra

AGE is more immediately life-threatening; it occurs when air bubbles embolize to the brain during rapid ascent. Both require hyperbaric oxygen therapy. Position supine, give high-flow O₂, transport to a hyperbaric facility.
