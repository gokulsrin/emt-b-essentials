## Text

For a drowning victim in cardiac arrest, begin with {{c1::5 rescue breaths::first step}} before starting chest compressions, because drowning arrest is primarily {{c2::hypoxic::mechanism}}.

## Extra

Unlike cardiac arrest from a primary cardiac cause, drowning arrest is driven by oxygen depletion. Early ventilation reverses the primary insult. Follow standard CPR after the initial rescue breaths. Always consider spinal injury if diving mechanism.
