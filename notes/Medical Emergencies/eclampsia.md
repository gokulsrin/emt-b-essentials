## Text

<b>Eclampsia</b> = preeclampsia + {{c1::seizure::defining event}}; position in {{c2::left lateral tilt::position}} to relieve aortocaval compression and protect the airway.

## Extra

Magnesium sulfate is the definitive anticonvulsant for eclampsia but is an ALS/hospital drug. EMT management is supportive: airway, O₂, left lateral position, calm environment, rapid transport. Do not restrain the seizing limbs.
