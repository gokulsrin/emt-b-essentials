## Text

<b>Ectopic pregnancy</b>: <u>missed period + unilateral pelvic pain + signs of shock</u> — treat as {{c1::hemorrhagic shock::diagnosis}} and transport rapidly; do NOT delay for vaginal exam.

## Extra

Ectopic pregnancy (usually in a fallopian tube) can rupture causing massive internal hemorrhage. Shock out of proportion to visible blood loss is a key clue. Prehospital treatment is supportive: O₂, position of comfort, rapid transport, ALS if available.
