## Text

<b>Excited delirium</b> triad: {{c1::extreme agitation}}, {{c1::hyperthermia}}, {{c1::superhuman strength}} — carries {{c2::high mortality::risk}} especially after physical restraint.

## Extra

Excited delirium is commonly associated with stimulant use (cocaine, methamphetamine) or acute psychiatric illness. Death can occur suddenly due to cardiac arrhythmia and hyperthermia. Request ALS and law enforcement; minimize physical struggle time.
