## Text

In <b>frostbite</b>, do NOT rewarm in the field if there is any risk of {{c1::refreezing::complication}} — thaw-refreeze injury is {{c2::worse than leaving frozen::comparison}}.

## Extra

Rewarming is appropriate only when the patient is in a warm environment and refreezing is impossible. Never rub frostbitten tissue; it causes mechanical damage to ice crystal–laden cells. Protect the area, remove wet clothing, and transport.
