## Text

<b>Upper GI bleed</b> presents as {{c1::hematemesis::vomiting blood}} (<u>bright red</u> or <u>coffee-ground</u>) and/or {{c2::melena::black tarry stool}}.

## Extra

Bright red emesis = active arterial bleed. Coffee-ground emesis = slower bleed with partial digestion. Melena results from digested blood passing through the colon. All are signs of significant blood loss; treat for shock.
