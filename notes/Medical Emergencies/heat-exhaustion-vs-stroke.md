## Text

<b>Heat exhaustion</b>: {{c1::heavy sweating, cool/pale/moist skin, weakness, normal or mildly elevated temp::presentation}}; <b>heat stroke</b>: {{c2::hot dry skin, AMS, temp &gt;104°F (40°C)::presentation}} — heat stroke is a life threat.

## Extra

The key differentiator is CNS status and skin. Heat stroke requires rapid cooling (ice packs to neck/axilla/groin, misting/fanning) and emergent transport. Heat exhaustion: move to cool environment, oral fluids if alert, rest.
