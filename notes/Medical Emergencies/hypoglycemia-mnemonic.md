## Text

Mnemonic: <u>"cold and clammy, give 'em candy"</u> → {{c1::hypoglycemia::condition}}; <u>"hot and dry, sugar's high"</u> → {{c2::hyperglycemia::condition}}.

## Extra

Cold/clammy skin in hypoglycemia reflects sympathetic activation (epinephrine surge). Warm/dry skin in hyperglycemia reflects dehydration from osmotic diuresis.
