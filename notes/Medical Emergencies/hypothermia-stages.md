## Text

<b>Hypothermia</b> stages: mild ({{c1::32–35°C::temp range}}) → {{c1::shivering, alert}}; moderate ({{c2::28–32°C::temp range}}) → {{c2::no shivering, AMS, bradycardia}}; severe (&lt;{{c3::28°C::temp}}) → {{c3::unconscious, possible arrest — handle gently}}.

## Extra

Loss of shivering in moderate hypothermia is ominous — the body's primary heat-generating mechanism has failed. Rough handling in severe hypothermia can trigger ventricular fibrillation. Rewarm passively; active rewarming requires hospital resources.
