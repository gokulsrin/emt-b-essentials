## Text

In a <b>lightning mass-casualty incident</b>, use <u>reverse triage</u> — treat {{c1::apparently dead patients first::who to prioritize}}, because lightning cardiac arrest victims have the best chance of survival with immediate CPR.

## Extra

Unlike most MCI scenarios where apneic/pulseless patients are tagged black, lightning victims can be resuscitated from cardiac arrest even after prolonged apnea. Those who are conscious and breathing are more stable. Standard MCI rules are inverted.
