## Text

After delivery, heavy postpartum bleeding is treated with {{c1::fundal massage::intervention}} to stimulate {{c2::uterine contraction::mechanism}}; simultaneously treat for shock and transport.

## Extra

Fundal massage stimulates uterine tone to control postpartum hemorrhage (uterine atony is the most common cause of PPH). Massage the uterus firmly through the abdomen in a circular motion. Also clamp and cut the cord: ~6 inches and ~9 inches from the baby, cut between clamps.
