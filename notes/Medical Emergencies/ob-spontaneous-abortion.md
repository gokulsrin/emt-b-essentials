## Text

<b>Spontaneous abortion</b> (miscarriage): vaginal bleeding + cramping before {{c1::20 weeks::gestational age}}; save and transport any {{c2::expelled tissue::what to save}} for hospital analysis.

## Extra

Do not tell the patient the baby is "lost" in the field — you cannot confirm this prehospital. Provide emotional support, treat for shock if hemorrhage, and transport. Tissue may be passed in the field; wrap in a moist dressing and bring to the hospital.
