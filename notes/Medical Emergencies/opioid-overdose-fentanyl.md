## Text

<u>Fentanyl and other synthetic opioids</u> may require {{c1::multiple doses::how many}} of <b>naloxone</b> to achieve reversal.

## Extra

Standard naloxone doses (2–4 mg IN) may be insufficient for high-potency synthetic opioids (fentanyl, carfentanil). Repeat dosing every 2–3 minutes if respirations do not improve. Some protocols allow up to 10 mg total.
