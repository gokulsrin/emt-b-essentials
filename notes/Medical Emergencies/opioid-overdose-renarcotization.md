## Text

<b>Naloxone</b>'s half-life is {{c1::shorter::shorter/longer}} than most opioids — the patient can {{c2::re-narcotize::complication}} after apparent recovery; transport even if they refuse.

## Extra

Naloxone half-life is approximately 30–90 minutes; most opioids last much longer. Patients who feel fine after naloxone can deteriorate again once it wears off. This is also called "re-sedation."
