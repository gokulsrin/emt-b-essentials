## Text

Classic <b>opioid overdose triad</b>:
{{c1::pinpoint (miotic) pupils}}, {{c1::respiratory depression (slow/shallow/apnea)}}, {{c1::decreased level of consciousness / coma}}.

## Extra

Respiratory depression is the killer — hypoxia from inadequate breathing is the primary cause of death in opioid overdose. Airway and ventilation (BVM) come before naloxone.
