## Text

<b>Placenta previa</b>: {{c1::painless bright red vaginal bleeding::presentation}}; <b>placental abruption</b>: {{c2::painful dark vaginal bleeding + rigid board-like abdomen::presentation}} — both are third-trimester emergencies.

## Extra

Previa = placenta covers the cervical os, bleeds with cervical dilation. Abruption = placenta separates prematurely, concealing blood behind it (dark blood or no visible blood). Neither should have a vaginal exam performed by EMTs. Treat for shock; transport.
