## Text

<b>Absorption poisoning</b> (organophosphates, skin/mucosa contact): decon by {{c1::brushing off dry powder first, then flushing with copious water for 15–20 minutes::decon steps}} and removing contaminated clothing.

## Extra

Brush before flush — water can activate some dry chemicals. PPE is essential for rescuers; secondary contamination is a real risk with organophosphates. Remove all contaminated clothing including shoes and jewelry.
