## Text

<u>"Flu-like symptoms in multiple household members"</u> without fever → think {{c1::carbon monoxide (CO) poisoning::diagnosis}}.

## Extra

CO is colorless and odorless; patients don't know they're being poisoned. Multiple victims in the same space sharing headache, nausea, dizziness, and confusion is the classic scene presentation. Scene safety: do not enter without confirmation of atmospheric safety.
