## Text

<b>Injection poisoning</b> (drugs, envenomations): keep the affected limb {{c1::at or below heart level::position}} and immobilize — do NOT apply {{c2::ice, suction, or "cut and suck"::contraindicated interventions}}.

## Extra

Dependent positioning slows venom spread. Ice causes vasoconstriction and can worsen local tissue injury. "Cut and suck" is ineffective and causes additional tissue damage. Constricting bands are generally not recommended either.
