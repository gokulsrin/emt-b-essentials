## Text

<b>Ingestion poisoning</b> (swallowed pills, plants, chemicals): identify the substance, amount, and time — bring containers. Activated charcoal is given {{c1::only per medical control::when}} and only if {{c2::recent ingestion, alert, no caustic/hydrocarbon, intact gag::criteria}}.

## Extra

Activated charcoal is not appropriate for caustics (acids/alkalis), hydrocarbons, or altered mental status. Always contact poison control / medical direction before giving.
