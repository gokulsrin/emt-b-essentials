## Text

<b>Preeclampsia</b>: {{c1::hypertension + proteinuria + edema::triad}} after {{c2::20 weeks::gestational age}} gestation — transport immediately; avoid stimulation.

## Extra

Classic warning signs include severe headache, visual disturbances ("flashing lights"), epigastric pain, and rapid weight gain. Preeclampsia can progress to eclampsia (seizure) at any time. Keep environment calm and dark; minimize lights and sirens when possible.
