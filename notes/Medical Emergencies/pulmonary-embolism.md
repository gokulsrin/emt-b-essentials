## Text

<b>Pulmonary embolism (PE)</b>: <u>sudden dyspnea + pleuritic chest pain + clear lung sounds</u>; key risk factor is {{c1::prolonged immobility (bed rest, long flight)::risk factor}}.

## Extra

Clear lungs distinguish PE from pulmonary edema (crackles) and pneumothorax (absent breath sounds). Tachycardia and hypoxia are common. Treat with high-flow O₂, position of comfort, and rapid transport; EMTs cannot confirm PE in the field.
