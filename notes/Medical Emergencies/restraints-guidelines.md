## Text

Physical restraints: use {{c1::4-point::type}} restraints only when necessary for safety, NEVER position the patient {{c2::prone::position}}, and {{c3::document::requirement}} the reason, method, and reassessments.

## Extra

Prone restraint causes positional asphyxia and is a documented cause of in-custody death. Reassess neurovascular status of restrained limbs frequently. Restraints require medical control authorization per most protocols.
