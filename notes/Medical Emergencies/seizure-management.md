## Text

During an active <b>seizure</b>, protect the patient from injury by {{c1::clearing the area and padding nearby surfaces::actions}} — do {{c2::not::do/do not}} restrain the limbs.

## Extra

Restraining seizing limbs can cause fractures and does not stop the seizure. Move hazards away from the patient; place soft padding under the head if possible.
