## Text

During an active <b>seizure</b>, protect the patient by {{c1::clearing the area and padding nearby surfaces::actions}} — do NOT restrain the limbs, as this can cause {{c2::fractures::complication}}.

## Extra

Restraining seizing limbs can cause fractures and does not stop the seizure. Move hazards away from the patient; place soft padding under the head if possible.
