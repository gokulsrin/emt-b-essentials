## Text

After a seizure ends, place the patient in the {{c1::recovery (lateral) position::position}} to protect the airway during the <b>post-ictal</b> phase.

## Extra

The post-ictal patient is unconscious or semi-conscious with depressed airway reflexes. Lateral positioning prevents aspiration if they vomit. Suction should be immediately available. Todd's paralysis (transient one-sided weakness) can mimic stroke in this phase.
