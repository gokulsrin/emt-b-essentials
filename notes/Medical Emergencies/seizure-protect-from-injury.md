## Text

After a <b>seizure</b>, check {{c1::blood glucose::test}} because {{c2::hypoglycemia::condition}} is a common precipitant.

## Extra

A low blood glucose can trigger seizures and should be corrected before transport if possible. Also check for: missed seizure medications, head trauma, fever (febrile seizure in peds), alcohol withdrawal, and structural CNS causes.
