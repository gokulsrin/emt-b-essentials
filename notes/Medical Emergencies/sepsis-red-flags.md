## Text

<b>Sepsis</b> red flags (cloze all): {{c1::fever OR hypothermia}}, {{c1::tachycardia}}, {{c1::tachypnea}}, {{c1::altered mental status}}, {{c1::hypotension}}.

## Extra

Hypothermia can occur in severe sepsis — do not be falsely reassured by a normal or low temperature. Hypotension is a late and ominous sign indicating septic shock. High-flow O₂ and rapid transport are priorities.
