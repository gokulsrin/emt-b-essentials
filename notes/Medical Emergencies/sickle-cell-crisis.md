## Text

<b>Sickle cell crisis</b> (vaso-occlusive): severe pain in the {{c1::bones, chest, or abdomen::locations}}; treat with {{c2::high-flow O₂ and analgesia::treatment}} and transport; watch for {{c3::acute chest syndrome::complication}} (chest pain + fever + hypoxia).

## Extra

Acute chest syndrome (ACS in sickle cell) is a pulmonary emergency similar to pneumonia/PE caused by sickling in pulmonary vasculature. It can be rapidly fatal. Triggers for crisis include dehydration, infection, cold, and hypoxia; avoid them.
