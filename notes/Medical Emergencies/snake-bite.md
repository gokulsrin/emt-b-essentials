## Text

<b>Snake bite</b> management: immobilize the limb at {{c1::heart level::position}} and splint; do NOT apply {{c2::ice, tourniquet, or suction::contraindicated}}.

## Extra

"Cut and suck" is ineffective and causes additional injury. Ice causes vasoconstriction, worsening local necrosis. Constricting bands are also contraindicated. Remove rings/jewelry proximal to the bite for anticipated swelling. Identify the snake if safe.
