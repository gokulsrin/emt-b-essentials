## Text

<u>Focal neurologic deficits</u> indistinguishable from stroke can be caused by {{c1::hypoglycemia::condition}} — always check {{c2::blood glucose::test}} before calling a stroke alert.

## Extra

A low blood sugar can produce focal weakness, slurred speech, and altered mental status that resolves with glucose correction. This is the #1 stroke mimic in the prehospital setting. If glucose is low, treat it; do not bypass to stroke center without first correcting hypoglycemia.
