## Text

In the prehospital setting, EMTs {{c1::cannot distinguish::ability}} ischemic from hemorrhagic stroke — this is why aspirin is {{c2::not given::action}} in the field for suspected stroke.

## Extra

Giving aspirin to a hemorrhagic stroke patient would worsen bleeding. Only CT imaging can differentiate ischemic from hemorrhagic stroke. Prehospital management is identical for both: Cincinnati screen, LKW time, O₂, rapid transport to stroke center.
