## Text

In an <b>active shooter / rescue task force</b> scenario, EMS operates in the {{c1::warm zone::zone}} alongside law enforcement; the concept is {{c2::care under fire::principle}} — provide only hemorrhage control while moving to cover.

## Extra

Full patient assessment and treatment begin only in the cold zone. Warm zone care is limited to tourniquet application, wound packing, and airway positioning. Never self-deploy into the hot (active threat) zone.
