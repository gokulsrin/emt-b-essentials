## Text

<b>Ambulance staging</b>: at potentially unsafe scenes (violent calls, active shooter), EMS waits at a {{c1::designated safe location::where}} until {{c2::law enforcement declares the scene secure::condition}}.

## Extra

Staging distance is typically one to two blocks or around a corner — close enough for rapid deployment but out of sight of the threat. Do not self-dispatch into an unsecured scene.
