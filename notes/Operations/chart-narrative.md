## Text

<b>CHART</b> narrative format (named list): {{c1::Chief complaint}}, {{c1::History}}, {{c1::Assessment}}, {{c1::Rx (treatment)}}, {{c1::Transport}}.

## Extra

An alternative to SOAP commonly used in EMS. Rx section covers all interventions performed on scene and en route. Transport section documents destination, mode (lights/siren or not), and patient condition changes during transport.
