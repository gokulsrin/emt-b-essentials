## Text

<b>Expressed consent</b>: a {{c1::competent adult::who}} verbally or in writing agrees to treatment after being informed of the risks and benefits.

## Extra

Expressed consent requires the patient to be alert, oriented, and capable of making decisions. It is the gold standard — always obtain it when possible.
