## Text

<b>Implied consent</b> applies when the patient {{c1::cannot consent (unconscious, AMS, unresponsive minor without parent)::condition}} and a {{c2::true emergency exists::requirement}}; the law assumes a reasonable person would want treatment.

## Extra

Also applies to a patient who is so intoxicated they cannot make decisions. Document why consent could not be obtained. Does not apply to non-emergency situations.
