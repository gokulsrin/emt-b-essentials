## Text

For a minor, consent is usually required from a {{c1::parent or legal guardian::who}}; <b>emancipated minors</b> ({{c2::married, in military, or court-declared::examples}}) may consent for themselves.

## Extra

In a true emergency when a parent cannot be reached, implied consent applies. Some states also allow minors to consent for specific services (STI treatment, mental health). When in doubt, treat and transport.
