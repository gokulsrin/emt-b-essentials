## Text

At a <b>crime scene</b>, EMTs should {{c1::disturb evidence as little as possible::evidence rule}} and {{c2::document the position of any items moved for patient care::documentation}}.

## Extra

Touch only what is necessary for patient care. Cut clothing along seams (not through bullet holes or stab wound patterns). Note and report observations (e.g., weapon location, patient's statements) — these may be legally significant.
