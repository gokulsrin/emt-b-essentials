## Text

<b>Due regard</b>: even with lights and siren active, an EMS driver must operate with {{c1::due regard for the safety of all others::standard}} and remains {{c2::liable for collisions::consequence}} caused by unsafe operation.

## Extra

Lights and siren grant limited exemptions (e.g., proceeding through red lights after stopping) but do not grant immunity from civil or criminal liability. Most EMS collisions occur at intersections.
