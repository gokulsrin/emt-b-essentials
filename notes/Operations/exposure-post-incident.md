## Text

After a potential infectious exposure, the EMT must {{c1::wash the exposure site immediately, report to a supervisor, and seek medical evaluation::immediate steps}} to allow source patient testing and prophylaxis if indicated.

## Extra

Time is critical for some exposures (e.g., HIV PEP must start within 72 hours). Document the incident with an exposure report. Follow agency and OSHA bloodborne pathogen exposure control plan procedures.
