## Text

<b>Hazmat cold zone</b> (support zone): the safe area where {{c1::EMS patient care, command post, and staging::activities}} occur. Patients must be fully decontaminated before entering.

## Extra

This is where standard EMS assessment and treatment begins. No contamination risk in the cold zone — standard PPE applies.
