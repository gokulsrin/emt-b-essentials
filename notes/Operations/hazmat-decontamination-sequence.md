## Text

<b>Decontamination</b> sequence: {{c1::gross decon (clothing removal and water rinse)::first step}} → {{c2::technical decon (detailed cleaning by hazmat team)::second step}}.

## Extra

Clothing removal alone eliminates up to 80% of contamination. Water rinse (copious, head-to-toe) follows. Technical decon uses brushes, soap, and specialized solutions. Patients must complete both steps before entering the cold zone.
