## Text

<b>DOT placard</b> colors (named list): {{c1::red = flammable}}, {{c1::yellow = oxidizer}}, {{c1::blue = water-reactive}}, {{c1::white = poison / inhalation hazard}}.

## Extra

Placards are diamond-shaped and displayed on transport vehicles. The UN number on the placard identifies the specific substance — look it up in the ERG (Emergency Response Guidebook) for isolation and PPE guidance.
