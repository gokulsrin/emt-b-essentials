## Text

At a <b>hazmat</b> scene, stage the apparatus {{c1::uphill, upwind, and upstream::position}} of the release.

## Extra

This positioning protects responders from vapors, runoff, and wind-driven contamination. Initial isolation distances come from the DOT Emergency Response Guidebook (ERG) — orange-cover handbook carried in every ambulance.
