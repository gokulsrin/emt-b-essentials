## Text

<b>Hazmat warm zone</b> (contamination reduction / decon corridor): {{c1::decontamination::activity}} happens here. Trained responders in appropriate PPE perform gross decon (clothing removal, water flush) before patients move to the cold zone.

## Extra

Some EMS systems use specially trained crews to support patient handoff at the warm/cold border. Patients must be decontaminated before leaving the warm zone.
