## Text

When approaching a helicopter, always approach from the {{c1::front::direction}} (where the pilot can see you) and never from the {{c2::rear (tail rotor)::dangerous direction}}.

## Extra

Stay low (crouch), approach only when signaled by the crew, and never approach the uphill side on sloped terrain — rotor clearance is reduced on the uphill side.
