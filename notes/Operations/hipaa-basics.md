## Text

<b>HIPAA</b> protects {{c1::Protected Health Information (PHI)::what}}; share only the {{c2::minimum necessary::principle}} for treatment, payment, or operations.

## Extra

Permitted disclosures without consent: treatment (handoff to hospital), payment (billing), operations (quality review). Public health reporting (mandatory reportable conditions) and law enforcement subpoenas are also permitted. Never share PHI with media, family (unless patient authorizes), or social media.
