## Text

In <b>ICS</b>, the {{c1::Incident Commander::role}} has overall authority and responsibility for the incident and reports to no one on-scene.

## Extra

The IC sets objectives, approves the Incident Action Plan, and is the only role that cannot be delegated away entirely. In small incidents the IC may fill all command staff roles.
