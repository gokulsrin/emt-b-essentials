## Text

<b>ICS span of control</b>: one supervisor should manage {{c1::3–7::number}} subordinates, with {{c2::5::number}} being optimal.

## Extra

Span of control prevents communication breakdown during large incidents. If a supervisor has more than 7 resources, an additional level of supervision (Division, Group, or Branch) must be created.
