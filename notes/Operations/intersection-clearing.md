## Text

At a controlled intersection with lights and siren, the driver must {{c1::come to a complete stop and clear each lane of traffic individually::procedure}} before proceeding.

## Extra

Do not rely on other drivers seeing or yielding to you. "Drive to the worst driver on the road" — assume no one hears the siren. Never race through intersections.
