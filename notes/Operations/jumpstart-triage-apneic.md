## Text

In <b>JumpSTART</b> pediatric triage, an apneic child with a palpable pulse receives {{c1::5 rescue breaths::intervention}}; if still apneic → tag {{c2::Black::color}}.

## Extra

This differs from START, which tags any non-breathing patient Black after one airway reposition. JumpSTART accounts for children's greater susceptibility to respiratory arrest from non-cardiac causes.
