## Text

<b>Body mechanics</b> lifting rules (3-rule set): {{c1::keep the load close to your body}}, {{c1::lift with legs not back}}, {{c1::never twist while lifting — pivot your feet}}.

## Extra

Every inch the load is held away from the spine multiplies the lever force on the lower back. Bending at the knees with back straight and head up transfers load to the larger leg muscles.
Back injuries are the leading cause of EMS provider disability — more than violence or vehicle crashes combined.
