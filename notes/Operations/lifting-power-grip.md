## Text

The <b>power grip</b>: palms {{c1::up::orientation}}, fingers and thumb in {{c1::the same direction}}, hands at least {{c1::10 inches apart::spacing}}.

## Extra

The power grip maximizes hand contact area and grip strength. Compare to a weaker "hook grip" where fingers alone bear the load.
