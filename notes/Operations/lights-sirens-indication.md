## Text

<b>Lights and siren</b> should be used only for {{c1::true emergencies::indication}}; non-emergency transports are conducted {{c2::without lights or siren::mode}}.

## Extra

Overuse of lights and siren desensitizes the public and increases collision risk. Medical direction or protocol determines what constitutes an emergency response. Using L&S inappropriately does not reduce legal liability.
