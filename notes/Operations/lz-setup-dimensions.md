## Text

<b>LZ</b> (landing zone) minimum size: {{c1::100 × 100 ft::dimensions}}; surface must be flat and clear of overhead wires, with {{c2::corners marked and wind direction communicated to the pilot::setup requirements}}.

## Extra

Mark corners with cones, chemical lights, or vehicles (lights off, facing inward so they don't blind the pilot). Communicate wind with a smoke flare or by pointing into the wind over radio.
