<b>ICS</b> command sections under the Incident Commander (named list): {{c1::Operations}}, {{c1::Planning}}, {{c1::Logistics}}, {{c1::Finance / Administration}}.

## Extra

Operations = tactical actions; Planning = resource tracking and IAP; Logistics = supplies, communications, facilities; Finance/Admin = costs, procurement, time recording. Each section is led by a Section Chief.
