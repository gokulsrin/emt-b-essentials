## Text

A <b>mental health hold</b> (involuntary transport) may be authorized by a {{c1::law enforcement officer or physician::who}} when a patient is a danger to themselves or others and refuses care.

## Extra

Specific criteria and hold designations (e.g., 5150 in CA, Baker Act in FL) are state-specific. EMTs act under the legal authority of the hold — document carefully. This is distinct from restraint, which may also be needed.
