## Text

A <b>non-urgent move</b> is used when {{c1::the scene is safe and no immediate life threat exists::condition}}; it allows full packaging and assessment before transport.

## Extra

Used when there is no immediate scene hazard or life threat. Allows time for spinal precautions, splinting, and proper stretcher loading. Always preferable when conditions permit.
