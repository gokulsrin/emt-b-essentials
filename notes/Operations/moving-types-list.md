## Text

Three <b>patient move</b> priority types (named list):
{{c1::emergent}}, {{c1::urgent}}, {{c1::non-urgent}}.

## Extra

Emergent = immediate threat to life or inability to reach the patient for care; urgent = life-threatening condition requiring move before full assessment; non-urgent = controlled, full packaging, the default.
