## Text

An <b>urgent move</b> is used when the patient has a {{c1::life-threatening condition that requires moving before full assessment or packaging::indication}}.

## Extra

Example: a patient in respiratory arrest needs to be moved to a hard surface for CPR, but the scene itself is not immediately dangerous. Faster than non-urgent but less rushed than emergent.
