## Text

<b>NIMS</b> stands for {{c1::National Incident Management System::full name}} and provides a {{c2::standardized framework for multi-agency incident response::purpose}}.

## Extra

NIMS mandates use of ICS and is required for agencies receiving federal preparedness funding. It ensures interoperability between local, state, and federal responders.
