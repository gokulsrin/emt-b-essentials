## Text

The <b>PCR</b> (Patient Care Report) is a {{c1::legal medical record::what it is}} and must be {{c2::accurate, complete, and objective::documentation standards}}.

## Extra

The PCR is used for quality improvement, billing, research, and legal proceedings. Errors should be corrected with a single line (never whiteout), initialed, and dated. Late entries must be labeled addenda.
