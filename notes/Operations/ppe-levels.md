## Text

<b>Hazmat PPE levels</b> (named list): {{c1::Level A = fully encapsulating suit + SCBA (highest protection)}}, {{c1::Level B = SCBA + chemical-resistant suit (no vapor seal)}}, {{c1::Level C = air-purifying respirator + chemical suit}}, {{c1::Level D = work uniform (no respiratory protection)}}.

## Extra

EMTs typically operate at Level C or D in the cold zone. Level A is for hot zone entry with unknown or high-vapor-pressure agents. Selection is based on the specific chemical, concentration, and route of exposure.
