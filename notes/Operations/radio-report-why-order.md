## Text

In an <b>EMS radio report</b>, leading with <b>ETA</b> and <b>chief complaint</b> allows the hospital to {{c1::mobilize resources (stroke alert, STEMI cath team, trauma team) before the patient arrives::reason}}.

## Extra

This is why unit ID, ETA, and chief complaint come first — not age or vitals. Resource mobilization decisions depend on the chief complaint, and the hospital needs maximum lead time to act on them.
