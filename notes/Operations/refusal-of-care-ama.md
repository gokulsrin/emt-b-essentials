## Text

For a valid <b>AMA</b> (against medical advice) refusal, the patient must demonstrate {{c1::decision-making capacity::requirement}}, have risks explained, and sign a {{c2::written refusal with a witness::documentation}}.

## Extra

Capacity = alert, oriented, and not impaired by drugs/alcohol/altered mentation. Always attempt to persuade, offer alternatives (transport to be evaluated), document thoroughly, and consider involving medical direction.
