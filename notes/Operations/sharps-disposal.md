## Text

Sharps must be disposed in a {{c1::puncture-resistant sharps container::container type}} immediately after use; never {{c2::recap needles::dangerous action}}.

## Extra

One-handed scoop technique is acceptable if recapping is absolutely necessary (e.g., drawing medications). Overfilled sharps containers are a significant needlestick risk — replace when 3/4 full.
