## Text

<b>SOAP</b> narrative format (named list): {{c1::Subjective}}, {{c1::Objective}}, {{c1::Assessment}}, {{c1::Plan}}.

## Extra

Subjective = patient's complaint in their own words; Objective = measurable findings (vitals, exam); Assessment = EMT's impression; Plan = treatments given and transport decision.
