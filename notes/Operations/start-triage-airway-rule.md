## Text

In <b>START</b> triage, apneic patient: reposition airway once — still apneic → tag {{c1::Black::color}}; breathing resumes → tag {{c2::Red::color}}.

## Extra

Repositioning the airway (head-tilt chin-lift or jaw thrust) is the only airway intervention performed during initial START triage. No BVM, no CPR during the triage sweep.
