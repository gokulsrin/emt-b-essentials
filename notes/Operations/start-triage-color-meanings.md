## Text

<b>START</b> triage color priorities (named list):<br>
<b>Red</b> = {{c1::immediate}}; <b>Yellow</b> = {{c1::delayed}}; <b>Green</b> = {{c1::minor / walking wounded}}; <b>Black</b> = {{c1::deceased / expectant}}.

## Extra

Red patients have life-threatening but salvageable injuries (severe respiratory distress, uncontrolled hemorrhage, shock).
Yellow = serious but stable (fractures, moderate burns, abdominal injuries without shock).
Green = ambulatory, minor injuries (lacerations, contusions).
Black = not breathing after one airway reposition, or injuries incompatible with life given available resources.
