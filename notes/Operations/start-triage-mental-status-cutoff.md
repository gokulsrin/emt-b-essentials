## Text

In <b>START</b> triage (Mental Status step): cannot follow simple commands → tag {{c1::Red::color}}; can follow simple commands → tag {{c2::Yellow::color}}.

## Extra

"Follow simple commands" is the Mental Status (M) step — the last step in RPM.
A patient who passes all three RPM steps (R <30, perfusion intact, follows commands) is tagged Yellow (delayed).
