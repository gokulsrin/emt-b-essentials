## Text

Normal adult <b>capillary refill time</b>: {{c1::< 2 seconds::threshold}}; prolonged CRT suggests {{c2::poor peripheral perfusion::significance}}.

## Extra

Assessed by blanching a fingernail and counting seconds to color return. Less reliable in cold environments or with peripheral vascular disease. Most useful as a trend in pediatric patients.
