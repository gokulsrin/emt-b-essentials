## Text

Normal adult <b>systolic blood pressure</b>: {{c1::90–140::mmHg}}; <u>hypotension</u> is operationally defined as SBP {{c2::< 90::mmHg}}.

## Extra

Trend matters more than a single number — a chronic-hypertension patient with a "normal" 110/70 may already be hypoperfusing. Always pair BP with mental status and skin signs.
