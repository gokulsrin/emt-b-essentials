## Text

Normal adult resting <b>heart rate</b>: {{c1::60–100::bpm}}.

## Extra

Bradycardia < 60 bpm; tachycardia > 100 bpm. Tachycardia is the first compensatory response to shock, pain, hypoxia, or fever.
