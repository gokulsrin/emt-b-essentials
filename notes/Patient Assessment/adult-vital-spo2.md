## Text

Normal adult <b>SpO₂</b> on room air: {{c1::95–100%::range}}; supplement O₂ when SpO₂ {{c2::< 94%::threshold}}.

## Extra

In COPD patients, target SpO₂ 88–92% to avoid blunting hypoxic drive. Pulse oximetry is unreliable with CO poisoning, severe anemia, nail polish, or poor perfusion.
