## Text

A drop in <b>AVPU</b> from Alert to V/P/U is one of the strongest signs of {{c1::decompensation::clinical significance}}.

## Extra

Causes of decompensating LOC: shock, head injury, hypoxia, hypoglycemia. Any deterioration in AVPU warrants immediate reassessment and escalation.
