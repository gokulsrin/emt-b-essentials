## Text

<b>AVPU</b> — quick level-of-consciousness scale:

<b>A</b> = {{c1::Alert}}, <b>V</b> = {{c1::responsive to Verbal stimulus}}, <b>P</b> = {{c1::responsive only to Painful stimulus}}, <b>U</b> = {{c1::Unresponsive}}.

## Extra

A&O × 4 = person, place, time, event. "A&O × 3" (person/place/time) is older shorthand still widely used. Document what the patient actually says, not just a letter score.
