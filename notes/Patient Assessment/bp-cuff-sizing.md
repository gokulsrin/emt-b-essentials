## Text

Correct <b>BP cuff</b> bladder width should cover approximately {{c1::80%::percentage}} of the patient's arm circumference.

## Extra

Cuff too small → falsely high reading; cuff too large → falsely low reading. Use thigh cuff for morbidly obese arms; pediatric cuff for small arms. Always note if a non-standard cuff size was used.
