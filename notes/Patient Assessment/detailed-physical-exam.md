## Text

The <b>detailed physical exam</b> is a thorough head-to-toe exam performed {{c1::en route to the hospital::when}} if time and patient condition allow, primarily for {{c2::trauma patients::patient type}}.

## Extra

Only performed after the primary survey and rapid trauma assessment are complete. Covers head, neck, chest, abdomen, pelvis, extremities, and back. Not routinely done on stable medical patients — the focused exam and OPQRST/SAMPLE are sufficient.
