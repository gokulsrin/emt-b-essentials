## Text

The <b>focused exam</b> for a medical patient is guided by the {{c1::chief complaint::driver}}, using <b>OPQRST</b> to characterize the complaint and {{c2::SAMPLE::mnemonic}} for background history.

## Extra

Exam is limited to body systems relevant to the complaint — e.g., chest pain focuses on chest/cardiorespiratory; abdominal pain on the abdomen. Contrast with rapid trauma assessment, which is always head-to-toe regardless of complaint location.
