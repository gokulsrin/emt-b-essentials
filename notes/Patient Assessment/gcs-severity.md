## Text

<b>GCS</b> severity buckets: {{c1::13–15::score}} = <b>mild</b> head injury; {{c2::9–12::score}} = <b>moderate</b>; {{c3::≤ 8::score}} = <b>severe</b>.

## Extra

GCS ≤ 8: anticipate airway compromise, consider ALS / advanced airway. "8 = intubate" is a common teaching phrase — beyond EMT scope but guides transport decision and ALS intercept.
