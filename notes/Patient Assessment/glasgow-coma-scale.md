## Text

<b>Glasgow Coma Scale</b> — total score range {{c1::3–15::range}}, summed across three components: <b>E</b> = {{c1::Eye opening}} (1–4), <b>V</b> = {{c1::Verbal response}} (1–5), <b>M</b> = {{c1::Motor response}} (1–6).

## Extra

Lowest possible score is 3, not 0 (1 + 1 + 1). Score the patient's best response in each category. Document component scores (e.g., E3 V4 M5 = 12), not just the total — much more useful for downstream providers tracking trends.
