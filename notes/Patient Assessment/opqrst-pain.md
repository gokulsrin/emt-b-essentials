## Text

<b>OPQRST</b> — pain history mnemonic:

<b>O</b> = {{c1::Onset}}, <b>P</b> = {{c1::Provocation / Palliation}}, <b>Q</b> = {{c1::Quality}}, <b>R</b> = {{c1::Region / Radiation}}, <b>S</b> = {{c1::Severity}}, <b>T</b> = {{c1::Time / duration}}.

## Extra

Quality words can be diagnostic: crushing/pressure → cardiac; tearing between shoulder blades → aortic dissection; ripping lower back → AAA; burning retrosternal → reflux but cannot rule out cardiac. Document the patient's own words in quotes.
