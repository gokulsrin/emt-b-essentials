## Text

Quick rule for minimum acceptable <b>SBP</b> in children aged 1–10 years: {{c1::70 + (2 × age in years)::mmHg formula}}.

## Extra

Example: a 4-year-old's minimum acceptable SBP = 70 + (2 × 4) = 78 mmHg. Below this threshold, consider decompensated shock and treat aggressively.
