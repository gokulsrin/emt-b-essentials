## Text

Pediatric <b>respiratory rate</b> normals by age (breaths/min):

Newborn = {{c1::30–60::br/min}}, Infant (1–12 mo) = {{c1::25–40::br/min}}, Toddler (1–3 y) = {{c1::20–30::br/min}}, Child (6–12 y) = {{c1::18–25::br/min}}, Adolescent = {{c1::12–20::br/min}}.

## Extra

Respiratory rate decreases as children age toward adult norms. A toddler breathing at 20/min would be concerning (too slow); an adolescent at 20/min is normal.
