## Text

Pediatric <b>systolic BP</b> normals by age (mmHg):

Newborn = {{c1::~70::mmHg}}, Infant (1–12 mo) = {{c1::~70–80::mmHg}}, Toddler (1–3 y) = {{c1::~80–95::mmHg}}, Child (6–12 y) = {{c1::~90–110::mmHg}}, Adolescent = {{c1::adult range::mmHg}}.

## Extra

Hypotension is a late, pre-arrest finding in children — do not wait for BP to drop before treating shock. Tachycardia and poor perfusion are earlier and more reliable warning signs.
