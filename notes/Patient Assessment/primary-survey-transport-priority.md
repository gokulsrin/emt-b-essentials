## Text

At the end of the <b>primary assessment</b>, make a <b>transport priority</b> decision: life-threat found → {{c1::load & go::priority term}}; stable, no life threat → {{c2::stay & play::priority term}}.

## Extra

Load & go (high priority): life threats found — airway compromise, major hemorrhage, unconsciousness, shock. Stay & play (low priority): stable patient, no immediate life threats. The decision drives scene time and destination.
