## Text

<b>Pulse quality</b> descriptors — strength: {{c1::bounding}}, {{c1::strong}}, {{c1::weak}}, {{c1::thready}}; rhythm: {{c1::regular}} vs. {{c1::irregular}}.

## Extra

Thready = barely palpable; suggests severe hypoperfusion or shock. Bounding = forceful, easily felt; suggests high cardiac output or fever. Irregular rhythm may indicate dysrhythmia — note and report to receiving facility.
