## Text

Normal pupils are <u>PERRL</u>: {{c1::Equal}}, {{c1::Round}}, {{c1::Reactive to Light}}; <u>anisocoria</u> (unequal pupils) is a {{c2::neurological::system}} finding.

## Extra

Unequal pupils suggest uncal herniation, direct eye trauma, or cranial nerve III compression from a mass lesion. Bilateral fixed and dilated pupils in a non-medicated, non-hypothermic patient is an ominous sign. Always document baseline and any changes.
