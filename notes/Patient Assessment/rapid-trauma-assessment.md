## Text

The <b>rapid trauma assessment</b> is a systematic {{c1::head-to-toe::direction}} exam applying <b>DCAP-BTLS</b> at each region, performed for patients with {{c2::significant MOI::indication}}.

## Extra

Sequence: head → neck → chest → abdomen → pelvis → extremities → posterior. Takes 60–90 seconds. Pair each extremity region with <b>PMS</b> (pulse, motor, sensation) distal to injury. Performed en route if patient is unstable.
