## Front

How often do you reassess a <b>stable</b> vs. <b>unstable</b> patient en route?

## Back

- <b>Stable patient:</b> every <b>15 minutes</b>
- <b>Unstable patient:</b> every <b>5 minutes</b>
- Always reassess immediately after any <b>intervention</b> to confirm it helped — or didn't.

## Extra

A reassessment includes mental status, ABCs, vitals, the chief complaint, and the effectiveness of every intervention. NREMT items often hinge on whether you reassessed after an intervention, not just on doing the intervention.
