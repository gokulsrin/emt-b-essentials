## Text

<b>Respiratory quality</b> assessment axes: {{c1::depth}} (adequate vs. shallow), {{c1::rhythm}} (regular vs. irregular), {{c1::effort}} (labored vs. unlabored), {{c1::accessory muscle use}}.

## Extra

Accessory muscle use (sternocleidomastoid, intercostals, abdominals) signals increased work of breathing — a pre-failure warning. Tripod positioning is a classic sign of severe respiratory distress.
