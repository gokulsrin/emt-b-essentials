## Text

<b>Skin color diagnostic</b>: <u>pale</u> → {{c1::vasoconstriction / shock / anemia::cause}}; <u>flushed</u> → {{c1::fever / heat / vasodilation::cause}}; <u>cyanotic</u> → {{c1::hypoxia::cause}}; <u>jaundiced</u> → {{c1::liver dysfunction::cause}}.

## Extra

Also assess temperature (cool/warm/hot) and moisture (dry/clammy). Cool, pale, clammy skin is the classic shock triad. Cyanosis of the lips and fingertips (central vs. peripheral) carries different significance.
