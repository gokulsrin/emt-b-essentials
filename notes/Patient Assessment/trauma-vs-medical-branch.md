## Text

After scene size-up, patient assessment branches on <b>MOI vs. NOI</b>: {{c1::significant MOI::trauma}} → <b>rapid trauma assessment</b>; {{c2::NOI (medical)::medical}} → <b>focused exam</b> by chief complaint.

## Extra

MOI = mechanism of injury (trauma); NOI = nature of illness (medical). The branch determines the order and scope of your secondary assessment. An unclear presentation defaults toward trauma protocols if any significant MOI is suspected.
