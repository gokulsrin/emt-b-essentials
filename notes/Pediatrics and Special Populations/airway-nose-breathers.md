## Text

Infants are <u>obligate nose breathers</u> until approximately {{c1::6 months::age}}; nasal congestion must be {{c2::suctioned / cleared::intervention}} before assessing respiratory effort.

## Extra

Young infants cannot easily switch to mouth breathing when the nose is blocked. Use a bulb syringe or suction catheter — clearing the nares often dramatically improves apparent respiratory distress.
