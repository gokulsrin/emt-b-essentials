## Text

Infants are obligate <u>nose breathers</u> until approximately {{c1::6 months::age}}; nasal secretions must be {{c2::suctioned / cleared::intervention}} to relieve respiratory distress.

## Extra

Unlike older children and adults, young infants cannot easily switch to mouth breathing when the nose is blocked. Bulb syringe or suction catheter before feeding or before assessing breathing effort.
