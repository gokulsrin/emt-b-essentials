## Text

To open a <b>pediatric airway</b> with a large occiput, place a {{c1::folded towel::device}} under the {{c2::shoulders::location}} to bring the airway into a <u>sniffing position</u>.

## Extra

In adults and older children, a towel goes under the head to achieve the sniffing position. In infants, the large occiput already flexes the neck — the towel must go under the shoulders to neutralize that flex and align the airway axes.
