## Text

<b>APGAR</b> acronym — 5 components scored at 1 and 5 minutes after birth:
{{c1::Appearance (skin color)}}, {{c1::Pulse}}, {{c1::Grimace (reflex irritability)}}, {{c1::Activity (muscle tone)}}, {{c1::Respirations}}.

## Extra

Each component is scored 0–2; total possible score is 0–10. APGAR is a documentation tool — never delay resuscitation to calculate the score. Most NRP decisions are made on HR and respiratory effort within the first minute.
