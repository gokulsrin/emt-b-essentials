## Text

<b>APGAR Appearance</b> (skin color) scoring: 0 = {{c1::blue/pale all over}}; 1 = {{c1::acrocyanotic (pink body, blue extremities)}}; 2 = {{c1::fully pink}}.

## Extra

Acrocyanosis (blue hands and feet with pink body) is common and normal in the first minutes of life. Central cyanosis (blue trunk and face) is always abnormal and requires intervention.
