## Text

<b>APGAR Grimace</b> (reflex irritability) scoring: 0 = {{c1::no response}}; 1 = {{c1::grimace only}}; 2 = {{c1::cry, cough, or sneeze on stimulation}}.

## Extra

Tested by suctioning the nose/mouth or flicking the sole. A vigorous cry on stimulation is the ideal response (score 2). No response at all is a serious sign.
