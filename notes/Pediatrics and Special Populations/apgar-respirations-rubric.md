## Text

<b>APGAR Respirations</b> scoring: 0 = {{c1::absent}}; 1 = {{c1::weak or irregular}}; 2 = {{c1::strong cry}}.

## Extra

A strong, vigorous cry is the gold-standard respiratory response. Gasping or irregular breathing scores 1 and typically requires stimulation and/or supplemental O₂. Absent respirations → begin PPV immediately.
