## Text

<b>APGAR</b> score interpretation: {{c1::7–10}} = normal; {{c1::4–6}} = moderately depressed (stimulate, BVM as needed, supplemental O₂); {{c1::0–3}} = severely depressed (full resuscitation — PPV, CPR if HR < 60).

## Extra

Score buckets guide the level of intervention, not the sequence. Even a score of 7 may require close observation if one component (e.g., HR) is borderline.
