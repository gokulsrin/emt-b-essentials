## Text

<b>APGAR</b> score interpretation: {{c1::7–10::score range}} = normal; {{c1::4–6::score range}} = moderately depressed → stimulate, O₂, BVM PRN; {{c1::0–3::score range}} = severely depressed → full resuscitation (PPV, CPR if HR &lt;60).

## Extra

Score buckets guide intervention level — never delay resuscitation to calculate the score. Even a score of 7 warrants close monitoring if a single component (especially HR) is borderline.
