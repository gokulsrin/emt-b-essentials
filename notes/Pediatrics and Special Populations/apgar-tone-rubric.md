## Text

<b>APGAR Activity</b> (muscle tone) scoring: 0 = {{c1::limp / flaccid}}; 1 = {{c1::some flexion of extremities}}; 2 = {{c1::active motion}}.

## Extra

A term newborn should have good flexion tone at rest. Limpness (hypotonia) is a key warning sign and correlates with other low APGAR scores.
