## Text

When caring for a patient with <b>autism spectrum disorder</b>: {{c1::stay calm and minimize stimuli::approach}}, {{c1::use simple direct language::communication}}, and {{c1::keep a familiar family member present::support}}.

## Extra

Avoid sirens, flashing lights, and physical contact unless necessary — sensory overload can trigger a behavioral crisis. Avoid restraints if at all possible. If the patient has a communication device, use it.
