## Text

Bariatric transport considerations: use a {{c1::bariatric stretcher::equipment}}; anticipate {{c2::obstructive sleep apnea::complication}} and {{c2::pressure-injury risk::complication}} with prolonged immobilization.

## Extra

Standard stretcher weight limits are typically 450–500 lbs; bariatric stretchers support 800+ lbs. Elevate the head of the stretcher to reduce work of breathing. Request additional personnel for safe lifting before arrival on scene.
