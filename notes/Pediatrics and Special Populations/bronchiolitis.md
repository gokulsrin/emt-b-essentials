## Text

<b>Bronchiolitis</b> is caused by {{c1::RSV (respiratory syncytial virus)::pathogen}}, affects children {{c2::&lt;2 years::age}}, and presents with <u>wheezing and nasal congestion</u>; EMT priority is {{c2::suction the nares::intervention}}.

## Extra

Bronchiolitis is the most common lower respiratory infection in infants. There is no bronchodilator or steroid benefit proven in the field — supportive care (suctioning, O₂, positioning) is the EMT role.
