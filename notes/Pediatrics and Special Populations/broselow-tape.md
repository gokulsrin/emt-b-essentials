## Text

The <b>Broselow tape</b> is a {{c1::length-based::basis}} tool that estimates a child's {{c2::weight and appropriate drug doses / equipment sizes::output}}.

## Extra

Lay the tape alongside the child from the top of the head to the heel. The color-coded zone provides weight estimate and corresponding medication doses, ET tube size, and defibrillation energy. Use it for every pediatric resuscitation.
