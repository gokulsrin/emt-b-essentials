## Text

The <b>TEN-4 bruising rule</b>: bruising on the <u>Torso, Ears, or Neck</u> in a child {{c1::&lt;4 years::age}} — or any bruising in an infant {{c2::&lt;4 months::age}} — is highly specific for {{c1::non-accidental trauma (child abuse)::diagnosis}}.

## Extra

Young children do not typically bruise in non-bony, padded areas without significant force. Document location, shape, and color of bruises. Report suspicion to medical control and law enforcement per protocol.
