## Text

<b>Croup</b> is a {{c1::viral::etiology}} upper airway infection presenting with a <u>barking cough</u> and <u>mild inspiratory stridor</u>; treatment is {{c2::humidified O₂ and a calm environment::treatment}}.

## Extra

Croup (laryngotracheobronchitis) typically affects children 6 months–3 years. The barking cough is pathognomonic. Agitation worsens stridor — keep the child calm and avoid unnecessary procedures.
