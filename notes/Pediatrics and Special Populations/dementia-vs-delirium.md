## Text

<b>Dementia</b>: {{c1::gradual, chronic, irreversible::onset/course}} cognitive decline; <b>Delirium</b>: {{c2::acute, fluctuating, potentially reversible::onset/course}} confusion with an identifiable cause.

## Extra

Delirium in an elderly patient is a medical emergency until proven otherwise — common causes include infection, medication changes, hypoxia, and metabolic disturbances. Do not attribute acute confusion to "their dementia."
