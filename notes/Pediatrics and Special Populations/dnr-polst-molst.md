## Text

<b>DNR / POLST / MOLST</b>: before honoring, confirm the document is {{c1::valid (signed, dated, present at scene)::criteria}}; when in doubt, {{c2::resuscitate::action}}.

## Extra

POLST (Physician Orders for Life-Sustaining Treatment) and MOLST (Medical Orders) carry the same legal weight as a DNR. Verbal statements by family are not sufficient — the physical document must be present. Follow local protocol and medical direction.
