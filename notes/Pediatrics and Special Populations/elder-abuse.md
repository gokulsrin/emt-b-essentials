## Text

Signs of <b>elder abuse</b> include <u>pressure ulcers, dehydration, and conflicting stories</u> between patient and caregiver; EMTs are {{c1::mandatory reporters::legal role}} of suspected abuse.

## Extra

Other red flags: unexplained injuries, poor hygiene, caregiver refusing to leave the patient alone, fearful demeanor. Document objectively and report to medical control and the appropriate authority per local protocol.
