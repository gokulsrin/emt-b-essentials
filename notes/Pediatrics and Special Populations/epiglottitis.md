## Text

<b>Epiglottitis</b> is a {{c1::bacterial::etiology}} airway emergency with <u>drooling, tripod positioning, dysphagia, and no cough</u>; EMT action is {{c2::do NOT agitate; do NOT use a tongue depressor; rapid transport::management}}.

## Extra

Classic presentation: "3 Ds" — Drooling, Dysphagia, Distress. Formerly caused by H. influenzae type b; now seen in unvaccinated children and adults. Any examination attempt can cause complete airway obstruction.
