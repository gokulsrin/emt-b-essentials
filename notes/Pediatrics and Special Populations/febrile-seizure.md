## Text

A <b>febrile seizure</b> typically occurs in children aged {{c1::6 months–6 years::age range}}, is {{c2::single, brief, and generalized::characteristics}}, and is triggered by {{c2::a rapid rise in fever::trigger}}.

## Extra

Simple febrile seizures self-terminate in &lt;5 minutes and are usually benign. Transport is indicated — the underlying cause of fever must be evaluated. Recurrence risk is ~30%. Status febrile seizure (&gt;5 min) requires ALS.
