## Text

The <b>posterior</b> fontanelle closes at approximately {{c1::2–3 months::age range}}.

## Extra

The posterior fontanelle is the smaller, triangular soft spot at the back of the skull. It closes much earlier than the anterior fontanelle and is rarely clinically relevant in EMS assessment.
