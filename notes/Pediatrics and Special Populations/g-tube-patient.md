## Text

A <b>gastrostomy tube (G-tube)</b> patient who appears bloated or distended may need the tube {{c1::vented (opened to relieve gas)::intervention}}; a dislodged G-tube is a {{c2::non-life-threatening::urgency}} issue but the tract begins closing within {{c2::hours::timeframe}}.

## Extra

G-tubes are used for long-term enteral feeding when oral intake is not possible. Do not forcefully reinsert a dislodged tube — cover the site with moist gauze and transport. Note the tube's French size for the receiving team.
