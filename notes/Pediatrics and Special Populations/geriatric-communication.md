## Text

When communicating with a geriatric patient who has <b>hearing or vision loss</b>: {{c1::speak slowly and face the patient::technique}}, and {{c1::transport glasses and hearing aids to the hospital::intervention}}.

## Extra

Do not assume altered behavior is the patient's baseline — sensory deficits can mimic confusion. Always confirm normal cognitive baseline with family or caregivers.
