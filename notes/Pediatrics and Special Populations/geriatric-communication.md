## Text

When communicating with a geriatric patient who has <b>hearing or vision loss</b>: {{c1::speak slowly and face the patient::technique}}, and {{c1::bring glasses and hearing aids to the hospital::intervention}}.

## Extra

Do not assume confusion is baseline — vision/hearing deficits can make a patient appear more altered than they are. Confirm the patient's normal cognitive baseline with family or caregivers when possible.
