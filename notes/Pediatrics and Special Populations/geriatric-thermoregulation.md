## Text

Elderly patients have <b>impaired thermoregulation</b> and can develop {{c1::hypothermia::condition}} in mildly cool environments; they are also at risk for {{c2::heat stroke::condition}} because {{c2::sweating is impaired::mechanism}}.

## Extra

Contributing factors include decreased subcutaneous fat, reduced metabolic rate, impaired shivering, and medications (beta-blockers, sedatives) that blunt compensatory responses. Always assess temperature and keep the patient covered.
