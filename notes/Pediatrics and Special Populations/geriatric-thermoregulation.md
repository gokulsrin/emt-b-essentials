## Text

Elderly patients have <b>impaired thermoregulation</b> and can develop {{c1::hypothermia::condition}} in mildly cold environments that would not affect younger adults.

## Extra

Contributing factors include decreased subcutaneous fat, reduced metabolic rate, impaired shivering, and medications (e.g., beta-blockers, sedatives) that blunt compensatory responses. Always assess temperature and protect from environmental extremes.
