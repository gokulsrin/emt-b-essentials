## Text

In an infant (&lt;1 year), the pulse is checked at the <u>brachial artery</u> on the {{c1::medial upper arm::location}} because the infant's {{c2::short, fat neck::anatomic reason}} makes the carotid difficult to palpate reliably.

## Extra

In children ≥1 year, use the carotid or femoral as in adults. Check for no more than 10 seconds — if HR &lt;60 with poor perfusion or absent pulse, begin CPR immediately.
