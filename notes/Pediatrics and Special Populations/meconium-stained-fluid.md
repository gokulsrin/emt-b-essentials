## Text

<b>Meconium-stained amniotic fluid</b>: if the newborn is {{c1::vigorous (crying, good tone, HR &gt;100)::status}} → routine care; if {{c2::depressed (limp, poor cry, HR &lt;100)::status}} → clear the airway, then begin {{c2::PPV::intervention}}.

## Extra

Vigorous newborns with meconium do not require aggressive suctioning beyond the mouth/nose. Depressed newborns require immediate airway clearance before PPV to prevent meconium aspiration syndrome.
