## Text

The first 30 seconds of newborn care: {{c1::dry}}, {{c1::warm}}, {{c1::stimulate}} (rub the back or flick the soles).

## Extra

This initial sequence is performed on every newborn regardless of appearance. If the newborn does not respond with improved color and respiratory effort after 30 seconds of stimulation, move to PPV.
