## Text

Newborn resuscitation: HR {{c1::&lt;100::threshold}} → begin <b>PPV</b>; HR {{c2::&lt;60::threshold}} despite PPV → add chest compressions at {{c2::3:1::ratio}} (compressions:ventilations) with {{c2::100% O₂::oxygen}}.

## Extra

Use a BVM with an appropriately sized mask. Ventilation rate during PPV is 40–60/min. Compressions use the two-thumb encircling technique at a depth of one-third of the chest diameter.
