## Text

<b>PAT Appearance</b> is assessed using the <b>TICLS</b> mnemonic:
{{c1::Tone}}, {{c1::Interactiveness}}, {{c1::Consolability}}, {{c1::Look / gaze}}, {{c1::Speech / cry}}.

## Extra

A child who is limp, uninterested in surroundings, inconsolable, has an abnormal gaze, or a weak cry has abnormal Appearance — a critical PAT finding regardless of work of breathing or skin color.
