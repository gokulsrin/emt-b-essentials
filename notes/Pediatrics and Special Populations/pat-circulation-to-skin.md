## Text

<b>PAT Circulation to Skin</b> — three abnormal findings:
{{c1::pallor}}, {{c1::mottling}}, {{c1::cyanosis}}.

## Extra

These reflect inadequate perfusion to the skin — a late and serious sign in children, who compensate well before decompensating rapidly. Mottling (irregular pale/purple patches) in a non-cold child strongly suggests shock.
