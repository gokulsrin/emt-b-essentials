## Text

The <b>Pediatric Assessment Triangle (PAT)</b> — 3 components assessed from the doorway:
{{c1::Appearance}}, {{c1::Work of Breathing}}, {{c1::Circulation to Skin}}.

## Extra

The PAT is a rapid, hands-off, 30-second "across the room" assessment. It does not require touching the child and provides the initial general impression before vitals or physical exam.
