## Text

<b>PAT Work of Breathing</b> — four abnormal signs assessed visually:
{{c1::abnormal sounds (stridor, wheezing, grunting)}}, {{c1::abnormal positioning (tripod, sniffing)}}, {{c1::retractions (intercostal, supraclavicular, sternal)}}, {{c1::nasal flaring}}.

## Extra

These signs indicate the child is working harder to breathe. Grunting is especially ominous — it reflects auto-PEEP and impending respiratory failure. Never force a child into a position they resist.
