## Text

<b>Mild pediatric dehydration</b> (≤5% fluid loss): child is {{c1::alert with slight thirst::presentation}}; fontanelle and mucous membranes are {{c2::normal::finding}}.

## Extra

At this stage, capillary refill is normal (&lt;2 s) and vital signs are maintained. Oral rehydration is appropriate if the child can tolerate fluids and is not vomiting.
