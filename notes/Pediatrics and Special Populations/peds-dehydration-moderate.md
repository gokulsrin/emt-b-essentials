## Text

<b>Moderate pediatric dehydration</b> (5–10% fluid loss): findings include <u>sunken fontanelle</u>, {{c1::dry mucous membranes}}, {{c1::decreased urine output}}, and {{c1::tachycardia}}.

## Extra

Capillary refill is borderline (2–3 s). The child may be irritable or lethargic. Transport for IV fluid resuscitation; note last void time when obtaining history.
