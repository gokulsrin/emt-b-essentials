## Text

<b>Severe pediatric dehydration</b> (&gt;10% fluid loss): findings include {{c1::lethargy}}, {{c1::no tears when crying}}, and <u>capillary refill &gt;3 seconds</u>.

## Extra

At this stage, hypotension may be present — a late and ominous sign in children. Treat as shock: high-flow O₂, position of comfort, immediate transport, ALS intercept if available.
