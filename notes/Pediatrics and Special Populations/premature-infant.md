## Text

A <b>premature infant</b> is born at {{c1::&lt;37 weeks::gestational age}}; key risks are {{c2::hypothermia, hypoglycemia, and fragile lungs / skin::risks}}.

## Extra

Thermoregulation is critical — premature infants lack subcutaneous fat and lose heat rapidly. Wrap in warm blankets; some protocols use plastic wrap for &lt;28-week infants. Handle with extreme care to avoid skin tears.
