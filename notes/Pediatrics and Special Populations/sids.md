## Text

<b>SIDS</b> (sudden infant death syndrome) is the sudden unexplained death of an infant {{c1::&lt;1 year::age}} during sleep; EMT actions are {{c2::resuscitate unless rigor/decomp and preserve the scene::management}}.

## Extra

SIDS is a diagnosis of exclusion made after autopsy and scene investigation. Do not disturb the scene unnecessarily. If obvious signs of death are absent, begin resuscitation. Provide family support and grief resources.
