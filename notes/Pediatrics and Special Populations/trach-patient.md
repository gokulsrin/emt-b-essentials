## Text

For a patient with a <b>tracheostomy</b>: ensure the stoma is {{c1::patent and unobstructed::status}}; suction {{c2::PRN::frequency}}; ventilate by placing the {{c2::BVM mask over the stoma::technique}} if the patient stops breathing.

## Extra

If the trach tube is obstructed and cannot be cleared by suctioning, remove the inner cannula if present, or the entire tube, and ventilate via the stoma opening. Always have suction available at the bedside.
