## Text

For a <b>ventilator-dependent</b> patient, bring {{c1::the home ventilator settings::information}} to the hospital; if the ventilator fails, ventilate manually with {{c2::a BVM::device}}.

## Extra

Obtain the rate, tidal volume, FiO₂, and any PEEP settings from the home vent or caregiver. If you cannot match the vent settings, manual BVM at a rate appropriate for age provides the bridge to definitive care.
