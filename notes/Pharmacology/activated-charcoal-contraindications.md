## Text

<b>Activated charcoal</b> contraindications: {{c1::altered mental status / no airway protection}}, {{c1::caustic ingestion (acids/alkalis)}}, {{c1::hydrocarbon ingestion (gasoline, kerosene)}}, {{c1::ingestion requiring endoscopy}}, {{c1::known allergy}}.

## Extra

Caustics and hydrocarbons are charcoal-specific contraindications — charcoal does not bind these agents, and vomiting/aspiration of caustics causes additional mucosal injury. When in doubt, contact poison control.
