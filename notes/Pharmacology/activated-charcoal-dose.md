## Text

<b>Activated charcoal</b> dose: {{c1::1 g/kg PO::dose/route}}, commonly {{c2::25–50 g adult / 12.5–25 g pediatric::typical amounts}}.

## Extra

Given orally (PO) — the patient must be awake and able to swallow. The charcoal physically adsorbs the toxin in the GI tract, preventing systemic absorption.
