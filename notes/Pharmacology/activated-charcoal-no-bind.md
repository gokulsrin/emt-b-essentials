## Text

<b>Activated charcoal</b> does <u>NOT</u> bind: {{c1::iron}}, {{c1::lithium}}, {{c1::alcohols (ethanol/methanol)}}, {{c1::heavy metals}}, {{c1::cyanide}}.

## Extra

Mnemonic: "I LACE doesn't go on charcoal" (Iron, Lithium, Alcohols, Caustics, Electrolytes/heavy metals). For these ingestions, activated charcoal is ineffective and specific antidotes or aggressive supportive care are required.
