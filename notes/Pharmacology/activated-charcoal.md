## Text

<b>Activated charcoal</b> is indicated for {{c1::recent oral ingestion of a charcoal-binding toxin (most pills, many plant toxins)::indication}}, given within {{c2::~1 hour of ingestion::timeframe}} per medical control.

## Extra

Use is increasingly restricted in modern EMS — many systems no longer carry it or require medical control authorization. Aspiration of charcoal causes severe pneumonitis. Always confirm with medical control or poison control before giving.
