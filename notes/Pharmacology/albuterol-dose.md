## Text

<b>Albuterol</b> doses: MDI — {{c1::2 puffs with spacer::dose/route}} vs. nebulizer — {{c2::2.5 mg in 3 mL saline over 5–15 min with O₂ at 6 L/min::dose/route}}.

## Extra

MDI with a spacer is preferred when the patient can cooperate with inhalation technique. Nebulizer is useful when the patient is too distressed to coordinate MDI use. Both deliver albuterol to the bronchial smooth muscle via inhalation.
