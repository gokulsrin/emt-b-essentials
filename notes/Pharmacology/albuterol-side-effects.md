## Text

<b>Albuterol</b> side effects: {{c1::tachycardia}}, {{c1::tremor/jitteriness}}, {{c1::anxiety}}, {{c1::headache}}, {{c1::mild hypertension}}.

## Extra

Tachycardia is the most clinically relevant — it can worsen demand ischemia in patients with CAD, and makes it harder to assess the patient's overall response to treatment. Relative contraindications: known albuterol allergy, symptomatic tachydysrhythmias, recent MI.
