## Text

<b>Albuterol</b> is a selective {{c1::β-2 agonist::receptor}} indicated for {{c1::bronchospasm/wheezing::indication}} from asthma, COPD, anaphylaxis (after epi), or reactive airway disease.

## Extra

Albuterol is assist-only in many EMS systems (patient's own MDI or prescribed nebulizer). Always reassess after treatment: lung sounds, RR, SpO2, and HR. A "quiet chest" with no audible wheeze is an ominous sign — the patient may be moving too little air to wheeze.
