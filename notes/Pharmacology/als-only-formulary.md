## Text

<b>ALS-only drugs</b> (EMT-B recognizes but does <u>NOT</u> administer): {{c1::epinephrine 1:10,000 IV/IO}}, {{c1::glucagon IM}}, {{c1::dextrose (D10/D50) IV}}, {{c1::atropine IV}}, {{c1::amiodarone IV}}, {{c1::midazolam IV/IM/IN}}.

## Extra

Knowing what ALS carries helps EMTs communicate drug effects, prepare syringes if authorized, and recognize when a patient has received ALS medications. Do not administer these without ALS licensure.
