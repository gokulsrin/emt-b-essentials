## Text

For <b>ACS</b>, aspirin must be <u>chewed</u>, not swallowed — {{c1::chewing::method}} produces {{c2::faster buccal absorption → faster platelet inhibition::reason}}.

## Extra

325 mg adult aspirin swallowed whole has significantly delayed onset vs. 4 × 81 mg baby aspirin chewed. The NREMT tests this distinction: method is part of the drug order, not optional.
