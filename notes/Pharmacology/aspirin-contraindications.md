## Text

<b>Aspirin</b> contraindications at the EMT level: {{c1::known ASA allergy}}, {{c1::active GI bleed}}, {{c1::suspected aortic dissection or hemorrhagic stroke}}, {{c1::age under 18 with viral illness (Reye's syndrome risk)}}.

## Extra

Suspected aortic dissection is a critical contraindication — aspirin's antiplatelet effect could worsen bleeding into the aortic wall. When chest pain is tearing/ripping and radiates to the back, do not give aspirin.
