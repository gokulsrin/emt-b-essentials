## Text

<b>Aspirin</b> works by {{c1::irreversible inhibition of cyclooxygenase (COX)::mechanism}} → reduced <u>platelet aggregation</u> and clot propagation.

## Extra

This is why aspirin is effective in ACS: it inhibits thromboxane A2 production in platelets, reducing the platelet plug formation that worsens coronary occlusion.
