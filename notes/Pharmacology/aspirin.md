## Text

First-line EMT drug for <b>suspected ACS</b> with chest pain: {{c1::aspirin (ASA)::drug}}.

## Extra

Aspirin is indicated for any chest pain of suspected cardiac origin when the patient has no contraindications. Even if the patient took aspirin earlier that day, give it if the prior dose was unclear or small — mortality benefit in true ACS is significant.
