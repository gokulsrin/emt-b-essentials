## Text

<b>Assist</b> = help the patient use {{c1::their own prescribed medication::ownership}} (e.g., nitroglycerin, albuterol MDI).<br>
<b>Administer</b> = give a drug you {{c1::carry on the unit per protocol::source}} (e.g., aspirin, naloxone, epinephrine auto-injector).

## Extra

If the patient does not have their own prescription, the EMT cannot assist — only administer from the unit formulary if indicated. Confirming a current, valid prescription is required before assisting.
