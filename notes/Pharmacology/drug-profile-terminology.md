## Text

<b>Drug profile terms</b>: {{c1::indication}} = reason to give; {{c1::contraindication}} = reason NOT to give; {{c1::side effect}} = expected unintended effect; {{c1::dose}} = amount and route.

## Extra

Every EMT drug card is organized around these four terms. Knowing them by name lets you parse protocols and medical control orders precisely.
