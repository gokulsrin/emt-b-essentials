## Text

<b>EMT formulary</b> — 8 medications all EMTs may administer or assist with:
{{c1::oxygen}}, {{c1::aspirin}}, {{c1::nitroglycerin}}, {{c1::oral glucose}}, {{c1::activated charcoal}}, {{c1::epinephrine}}, {{c1::albuterol}}, {{c1::naloxone}}.

## Extra

Exact scope, doses, and route restrictions vary by state and local protocol. Naloxone may be intranasal-only in some systems; epi may be auto-injector only; nitro and albuterol are often assist-only (patient's own prescription). Know your local protocol.
