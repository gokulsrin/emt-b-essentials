## Text

<b>Epinephrine auto-injector technique</b>: inject into the {{c1::lateral thigh::site}} (through clothing is OK), hold {{c1::3 seconds::hold time}}, massage site {{c1::10 seconds::massage time}}, then document the {{c1::time of administration::documentation}}.

## Extra

The lateral thigh (vastus lateralis) is preferred over the deltoid for faster absorption. Massaging the site disperses the drug into the tissue. The spent injector is a sharps biohazard — bring it to the ED.
