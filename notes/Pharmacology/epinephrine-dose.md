## Text

<b>Epinephrine</b> auto-injector doses: adult {{c1::0.3 mg IM::dose}} vs. pediatric (approx. 15–30 kg) {{c2::0.15 mg IM (Jr. auto-injector)::dose}}.

## Extra

Both are injected into the lateral thigh (vastus lateralis) — can be given through clothing. Hold in place 3–10 seconds then massage the site. May repeat every 5–15 minutes per protocol if symptoms persist. The spent injector is a sharps biohazard.
