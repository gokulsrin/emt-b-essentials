## Text

<b>Epinephrine</b> receptor effects: {{c1::α-1 → vasoconstriction}}, {{c1::β-1 → ↑ HR and contractility}}, {{c1::β-2 → bronchodilation}}.

## Extra

In anaphylaxis, all three effects are therapeutic: vasoconstriction reverses distributive shock, increased cardiac output supports perfusion, and bronchodilation opens the narrowed airway. This is why epinephrine is the only first-line drug for anaphylaxis.
