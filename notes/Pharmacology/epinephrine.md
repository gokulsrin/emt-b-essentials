## Text

<b>Epinephrine</b> auto-injector at the EMT level is indicated for: {{c1::anaphylaxis (or severe allergic reaction with airway or cardiovascular compromise)::indication}}.

## Extra

At the EMT scope, epinephrine auto-injectors are not used for routine cardiac arrest — that is an ALS intervention. Do not withhold epinephrine for fear of side effects in elderly or cardiac patients: anaphylactic death is worse than the risk of tachyarrhythmia.
