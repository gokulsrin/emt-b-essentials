## Text

<b>6 rights of medication administration</b>: {{c1::Right Patient}}, {{c1::Right Drug}}, {{c1::Right Dose}}, {{c1::Right Route}}, {{c1::Right Time}}, {{c1::Right Documentation}}.

## Extra

Verify all six before every drug given or assisted. "Right Documentation" is sometimes listed as the 6th right; some curricula list only 5 (omitting Documentation). NREMT expects you to know at minimum the first five.
