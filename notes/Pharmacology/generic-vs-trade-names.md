## Text

<b>Generic name</b> = {{c1::chemical (nonproprietary) name, lowercase::definition}} (e.g., epinephrine).<br>
<b>Trade name</b> = {{c2::brand name, capitalized::definition}} (e.g., EpiPen).

## Extra

The same drug always has one generic name but may have many trade names. Protocols use generic names; patients often know only the trade name. EMTs must recognize both.
