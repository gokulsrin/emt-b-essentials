## Text

Oral glucose gel is placed {{c1::between the cheek and gum::location}} and is <u>NEVER</u> given to a {{c1::unconscious patient::contraindication}} due to {{c2::aspiration risk::reason}}.

## Extra

An unconscious patient cannot protect their airway — glucose gel can obstruct or be aspirated. For unconscious hypoglycemia, request ALS (IV dextrose or IM glucagon) and protect the airway.
