## Text

<b>MDI technique</b> (with spacer): {{c1::shake}}, {{c1::exhale fully}}, {{c1::press canister + inhale slowly}}, {{c1::hold breath 10 seconds}}, {{c1::repeat after 1 min if ordered}}.

## Extra

A spacer improves drug delivery to the lower airways by eliminating the need for perfect coordination between pressing and inhaling. Without a spacer, most of the drug deposits in the oropharynx.
