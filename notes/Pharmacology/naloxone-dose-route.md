## Text

<b>Naloxone</b> EMT route in most systems: {{c1::intranasal (IN), 2–4 mg split between nostrils::dose/route}}, with onset in {{c2::2–5 minutes::onset}}.

## Extra

May also be given IM via auto-injector or IM injection per local scope. Repeat every 2–3 minutes if respiratory depression persists. IN delivery via atomizer device — half the dose in each nostril. Fentanyl/synthetic opioids may require multiple repeat doses.
