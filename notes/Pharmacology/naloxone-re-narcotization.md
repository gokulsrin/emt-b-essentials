## Text

<b>Naloxone</b> half-life ({{c1::30–90 min::duration}}) is <u>shorter than most opioids</u> → patient can {{c2::re-narcotize::risk}} after initial response; always transport even if they refuse.

## Extra

This is why "wake and bake" patients who refuse transport are dangerous — the naloxone wears off and the opioid effect returns, potentially causing respiratory arrest without anyone present. Document refusal carefully if the patient refuses AMA.
