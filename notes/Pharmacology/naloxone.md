## Text

<b>Naloxone</b> is an <b>opioid receptor antagonist</b> indicated for {{c1::suspected opioid overdose with respiratory depression or unresponsiveness::indication}}.

## Extra

Always ventilate first — naloxone fixes the cause but BVM with O2 fixes the dying patient immediately. Fentanyl and synthetic opioids often require multiple doses of naloxone due to potency.
