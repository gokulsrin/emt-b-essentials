## Text

<b>Nitroglycerin</b> is absolutely contraindicated if the patient took a <b>PDE-5 inhibitor</b> within {{c1::24 hours for sildenafil/vardenafil::timeframe 1}} or {{c2::48–72 hours for tadalafil::timeframe 2}}.

## Extra

PDE-5 inhibitors (sildenafil/Viagra, vardenafil/Levitra, tadalafil/Cialis) combined with nitrates cause refractory hypotension that can be lethal. Always ask specifically by drug name before assisting with nitroglycerin for chest pain. Other absolute contraindications: suspected right-sided MI, hypotension (SBP less than 90–100), head injury.
