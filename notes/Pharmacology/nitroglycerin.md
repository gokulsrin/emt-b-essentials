## Text

<b>Nitroglycerin</b> (patient's own prescription) is indicated for: {{c1::ischemic chest pain (suspected ACS)::indication 1}} and {{c1::acute pulmonary edema (CHF)::indication 2}} per protocol.

## Extra

EMTs assist with the patient's own prescription — nitroglycerin is not typically stocked and self-administered by the EMT. Confirm the patient has a current, valid prescription before assisting.
