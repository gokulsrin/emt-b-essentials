## Text

<b>Oral glucose</b> dose: {{c1::15 g glucose gel tube::dose}} administered by {{c2::squeezing between cheek and gum::method}}, then recheck glucose/mental status in {{c2::10–15 minutes::timing}}.

## Extra

Equivalent forms include juice, regular soda, or glucose tablets if the patient is cooperative. Repeat a dose if still symptomatic and able to swallow. Recheck both glucose level and mental status after each dose.
