## Text

An unconscious hypoglycemic patient who cannot protect their airway needs {{c1::IV dextrose or IM glucagon::ALS treatment}} — <b>oral glucose is contraindicated</b>.

## Extra

This is a key EMT scope-of-practice boundary. The EMT's role for the unconscious hypoglycemic is: protect the airway, provide supplemental oxygen, request ALS intercept, and transport rapidly. Do not attempt to administer anything orally to an unresponsive patient.
