## Text

<b>Standing orders</b> = protocols that allow drug administration {{c1::without contacting medical control (offline)::requirement}}.<br>
<b>Online medical control</b> = real-time {{c1::physician authorization by radio or phone::requirement}} before giving the drug.

## Extra

Most systems use standing orders for first-line drugs (aspirin, naloxone, epinephrine auto-injector) and online medical control for second doses, out-of-protocol situations, or assisted medications (nitroglycerin).
