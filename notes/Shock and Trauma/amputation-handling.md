## Text

An amputated part should be wrapped in {{c1::dry sterile gauze::wrapping}}, placed in a {{c1::plastic bag::container}}, and kept {{c1::on ice — never directly on ice::cooling method}}.

## Extra

Direct contact with ice causes frostbite to the tissue. The goal is cooling (4°C) to reduce metabolism and extend reimplantation viability to 6 hours for digits, less for larger parts. Always control hemorrhage at the stump first.
