## Text

<b>Beck's triad</b> (cardiac tamponade): {{c1::JVD}}, {{c1::hypotension}}, {{c1::muffled heart sounds}}.

## Extra

Cardiac tamponade is blood accumulating in the pericardial sac, compressing the heart. Beck's triad is the classic presentation; tracheal deviation is absent (distinguishes from tension PTX).
