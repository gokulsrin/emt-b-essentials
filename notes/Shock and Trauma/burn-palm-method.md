## Text

<b>Palm method</b>: the patient's palm (with fingers) equals approximately {{c1::1%::percentage}} TBSA.

## Extra

Used for irregular, scattered, or small burns where the Rule of Nines is impractical. Use the patient's own hand — not the provider's — because hand-to-body ratio is relatively consistent regardless of body size.
