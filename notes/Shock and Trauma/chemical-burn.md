## Text

For a <b>chemical burn</b> with dry powder: {{c1::brush off the dry powder first::first step}}, then irrigate copiously with water.

## Extra

Adding water to a dry reactive chemical (e.g., dry lime) can trigger an exothermic reaction and worsen the burn. Brush away the dry material first, then irrigate. Liquid chemical burns go straight to irrigation. Wear appropriate PPE and consider scene safety.
