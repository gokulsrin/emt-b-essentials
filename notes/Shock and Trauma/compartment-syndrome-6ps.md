## Text

<b>Compartment syndrome</b> — the <u>6 P's</u>: {{c1::Pain (out of proportion)}}, {{c1::Pallor}}, {{c1::Paresthesia}}, {{c1::Paralysis}}, {{c1::Pulselessness}}, {{c1::Poikilothermia (cool extremity)}}.

## Extra

Pain with passive stretch of the muscles in the compartment is the earliest and most reliable sign. Pulselessness is a very late finding. Field treatment: loosen/remove constrictive dressings, splints, or clothing; immediate transport.
