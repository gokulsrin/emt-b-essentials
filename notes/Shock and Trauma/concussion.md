## Text

<b>Concussion</b>: {{c1::transient AMS::consciousness change}} after head trauma, with possible {{c1::post-traumatic amnesia (PTA)::memory finding}}, and {{c1::no structural lesion::imaging finding}}.

## Extra

Loss of consciousness is not required for the diagnosis. Common symptoms include headache, nausea, dizziness, and light sensitivity. Transport for evaluation; do not clear for return to activity in the field.
