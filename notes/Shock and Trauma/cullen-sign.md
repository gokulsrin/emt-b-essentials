## Text

<u>Cullen's sign</u>: {{c1::periumbilical bruising::location and finding}} → retroperitoneal hemorrhage.

## Extra

Cullen's sign appears hours to days after injury as blood tracks along fascial planes to the umbilicus. It indicates significant retroperitoneal bleeding (pancreas, aorta, kidney). Transport immediately.
