## Text

<b>Cushing's triad</b> (rising ICP): {{c1::bradycardia}}, {{c1::hypertension with widening pulse pressure}}, {{c1::irregular respirations}}.

## Extra

Cushing's triad is a late, ominous sign of brain herniation. Treat as immediate transport; do not hyperventilate unless active herniation is suspected (paramedic-scope).
