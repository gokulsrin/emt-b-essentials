## Text

A <b>dental avulsion</b>: handle the tooth by the {{c1::crown::part}}, never the root; transport in {{c1::milk or the patient's own saliva::storage medium}}.

## Extra

The goal is to preserve the periodontal ligament cells on the root for reimplantation. Replanting the tooth into the socket immediately (if the patient is conscious and not at aspiration risk) is ideal. Do not scrub, dry, or wrap in dry gauze.
