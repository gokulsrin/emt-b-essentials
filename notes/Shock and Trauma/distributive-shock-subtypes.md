## Text

<b>Distributive shock</b> subtypes: {{c1::septic}}, {{c1::anaphylactic}}, {{c1::neurogenic}}, {{c1::psychogenic}}.

## Extra

All four involve loss of vascular tone (vasodilation) or vessel permeability, reducing effective circulating volume despite normal or elevated total blood volume. Psychogenic (simple fainting) is the mildest; septic carries the highest mortality.
