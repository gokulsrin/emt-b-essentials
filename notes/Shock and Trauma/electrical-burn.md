## Text

<b>Electrical burns</b> produce entry and exit wounds with {{c1::hidden internal damage along the conduction path::key concern}}; monitor for {{c1::arrhythmia::cardiac complication}} and {{c1::rhabdomyolysis::muscle complication}}.

## Extra

The skin wound grossly underestimates internal injury — current travels the path of least resistance through neurovascular bundles. Scene safety first: ensure power is off before approaching. Spinal precautions if the patient fell or was thrown.
