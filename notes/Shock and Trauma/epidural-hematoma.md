## Text

<b>Epidural hematoma</b>: arterial bleed ({{c1::middle meningeal artery::vessel}}); classic presentation is a <u>lucid interval</u> followed by {{c2::rapid deterioration::course}}.

## Extra

The biconvex (lens-shaped) hematoma on CT is pathognomonic. The lucid interval occurs because arterial bleeding is initially slow enough for the brain to compensate. Rapid neurosurgical evacuation is the only treatment.
