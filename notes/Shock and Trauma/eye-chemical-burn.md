## Text

<b>Chemical eye burn</b>: irrigate with water or saline for {{c1::≥ 20 minutes::duration}}, beginning <u>en route</u> to the hospital.

## Extra

Do not delay transport to complete irrigation on scene — continue irrigating during transport. Alkali burns penetrate more deeply and are generally more severe than acid burns. Remove contact lenses before irrigation if possible.
