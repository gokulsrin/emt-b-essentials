## Text

For an <b>embedded eye foreign body</b>: do {{c1::not remove::action}}; cover {{c1::both eyes::which eyes}} with a cup over the affected eye to avoid pressure.

## Extra

Covering both eyes eliminates sympathetic movement. A paper or plastic cup stabilizes the object without pressing on it. Non-embedded superficial particles may be gently flushed, but embedded objects must not be disturbed.
