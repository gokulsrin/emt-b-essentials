## Text

<b>Eye globe rupture</b>: apply {{c1::no pressure::pressure status}}; cover {{c1::both eyes::which eyes}}; position {{c1::supine::position}}.

## Extra

Covering both eyes reduces sympathetic movement (conjugate gaze) that could worsen injury. A rigid eye shield (not a patch) is preferred over the injured eye — nothing should touch the globe. Pressure from a patch can extrude vitreous humor.
