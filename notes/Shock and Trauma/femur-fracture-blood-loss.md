## Text

A closed <b>femur fracture</b> can cause {{c1::1–1.5 L::volume}} of internal blood loss into the thigh compartment.

## Extra

High-energy MOI (MVC, fall from height) is the typical mechanism in adults. Even without external bleeding, a bilateral femur fracture can alone cause hemorrhagic shock.
