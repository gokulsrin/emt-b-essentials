## Text

<b>Flail chest</b>: {{c1::two or more adjacent ribs::segment definition}}, each fractured in {{c2::two or more places::number of fractures}}, creating a mechanically detached segment.

## Extra

The segment is no longer coupled to the thoracic cage — it moves freely. The underlying lung is almost always contused, which drives morbidity more than the rib fractures themselves.
