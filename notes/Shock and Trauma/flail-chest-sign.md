## Text

Hallmark sign of <b>flail chest</b>: <u>paradoxical chest movement</u> — the flail segment moves {{c1::inward during inspiration and outward during expiration::direction}}, opposite the rest of the chest.

## Extra

"Paradoxical" = moves opposite to the expected direction. This happens because the detached segment responds to intrapleural pressure rather than to the chest wall muscles.
