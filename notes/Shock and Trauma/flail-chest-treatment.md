## Text

EMT treatment of <b>flail chest</b> with inadequate breathing: assist ventilations with {{c1::BVM::device}}, which <u>internally splints</u> the segment via positive pressure.

## Extra

Do not strap or sandbag the flail segment — this restricts ventilation further. Allow position of comfort. High-flow O2 for adequate breathing; switch to BVM if inadequate. Rapid transport to trauma center; ALS intercept. Mortality is driven by the underlying pulmonary contusion — watch for worsening hypoxia over time.
