## Text

<u>Grey-Turner's sign</u>: {{c1::flank bruising::location and finding}} → retroperitoneal hemorrhage.

## Extra

Like Cullen's sign, Grey-Turner's sign is delayed (hours to days post-injury) as blood dissects along fascial planes to the flanks. Both signs indicate severe retroperitoneal bleeding requiring immediate transport.
