## Text

<b>External hemorrhage control</b> sequence (named protocol):
{{c1::direct pressure}}, {{c1::wound packing}}, {{c1::pressure dressing}}, {{c1::tourniquet}}.

## Extra

Direct pressure: gloved hand or trauma dressing.
Wound packing: hemostatic gauze packed tightly to the bleeding source, held for ~3 minutes; used for deep junctional or extremity wounds.
Pressure dressing: bandage that maintains direct pressure hands-free.
Tourniquet: for life-threatening extremity bleeding uncontrolled by the above.
