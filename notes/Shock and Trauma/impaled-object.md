## Text

For an <b>impaled object</b>: {{c1::stabilize in place — do NOT remove::action}}; exception: object in the {{c2::cheek or airway::location}} that threatens the airway.

## Extra

Removing an impaled object can cause massive hemorrhage from a tamponaded vessel. Stabilize with bulky dressings around the object. If impaled in the cheek and blocking the airway, removal may be necessary with suction immediately available.
