## Text

Signs of <b>inhalation burn</b>: {{c1::singed nasal hairs}}, {{c1::soot in airway or sputum}}, {{c1::hoarseness or stridor::airway sound}} → initiate {{c1::early airway control::priority}}.

## Extra

Airway edema from inhalation injury can close the airway within minutes to hours. Hoarseness and stridor are red flags requiring immediate intervention. High-flow O2 for all suspected inhalation injuries; BVM or ALS airway if inadequate breathing.
