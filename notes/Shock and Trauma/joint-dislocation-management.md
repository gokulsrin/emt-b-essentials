## Text

For a <b>joint dislocation</b>, splint {{c1::in the position found::position}}, unless {{c2::distal CSM is compromised::exception condition}}.

## Extra

Attempting reduction in the field risks fracturing the bone, damaging neurovascular structures, or entrapping soft tissue. The exception — compromised pulse, motor, or sensory distal to the dislocation — requires medical direction and is rare at the EMT-B level.
