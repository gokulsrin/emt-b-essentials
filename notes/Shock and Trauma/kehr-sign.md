## Text

<u>Kehr's sign</u>: {{c1::left shoulder pain::location}} referred from {{c1::splenic rupture::source}}.

## Extra

Blood from a ruptured spleen pools under the left hemidiaphragm and irritates the phrenic nerve (C3-5), causing referred pain to the left shoulder. Mechanism: left upper quadrant trauma or spontaneous rupture (infectious mononucleosis).
