## Text

<b>Neurogenic shock</b>: {{c1::hemodynamic instability (hypotension + bradycardia) from loss of sympathetic tone::definition}}.<br><b>Spinal shock</b>: {{c2::transient flaccid paralysis and areflexia immediately after SCI::definition}}.

## Extra

These are distinct entities. Neurogenic shock involves the cardiovascular system; spinal shock involves the nervous system. Spinal shock resolves over hours to days; return of the bulbocavernosus reflex marks its resolution.
