## Text

<b>Obstructive shock</b> subtypes: {{c1::cardiac tamponade}}, {{c1::tension pneumothorax}}, {{c1::massive pulmonary embolism}}.

## Extra

All three physically impede blood flow into or through the heart. Tension PTX and tamponade both produce JVD; massive PE may not. EMT treatment is supportive (O2, BVM, rapid transport) — definitive interventions are out-of-hospital ALS or in-hospital.
