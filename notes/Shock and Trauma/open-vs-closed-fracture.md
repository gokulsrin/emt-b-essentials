## Text

<b>Open fracture</b>: {{c1::bone exposed through skin::defining feature}}; cover with {{c1::sterile dressing::treatment}}.<br><b>Closed fracture</b>: {{c2::skin intact::defining feature}}; splint as found.

## Extra

Open fractures carry a high infection risk. Do not attempt to push bone ends back — cover, splint, and transport. Even a small wound near a fracture site must be treated as open until proven otherwise.
