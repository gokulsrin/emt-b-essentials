## Text

<b>Parkland formula</b> (paramedic/hospital): {{c1::4 mL × kg × % TBSA::formula}} of LR over 24 hours; {{c1::half::fraction}} given in the first 8 hours.

## Extra

EMT-Bs do not calculate or initiate Parkland fluids — this is paramedic/hospital scope. Recognition of the formula supports triage communication and handoff. Superficial burns are excluded from the TBSA calculation.
