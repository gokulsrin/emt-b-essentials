## Text

A <b>pelvic binder</b> is placed at the level of the {{c1::greater trochanters::landmark}}, <u>not</u> the iliac crests.

## Extra

Placing at the greater trochanters closes the posterior pelvic ring and reduces pelvic volume. Placement at the iliac crests is ineffective and can distract the fracture. Apply over clothing if available.
