## Text

<b>Pelvic fracture</b> patterns: {{c1::open-book::lateral force splays ring open}}, {{c1::lateral compression::most common; ring buckles inward}}, {{c1::vertical shear::axial force displaces one hemipelvis superiorly}}.

## Extra

Open-book fractures carry the highest hemorrhage risk because the pelvic ring is unstable and the pelvic volume increases. A pelvic binder reduces volume and tamponades bleeding for open-book and some lateral-compression injuries.
