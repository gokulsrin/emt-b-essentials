## Text

<b>Pulmonary contusion</b>: bruised lung parenchyma causing {{c1::progressive hypoxia::symptom}} that appears {{c1::hours after::timing}} blunt chest trauma.

## Extra

Signs may be absent or minimal on initial assessment — hypoxia typically worsens over 12–24 hours as edema fills alveoli. High-flow O2 and BVM support as needed; closely monitor SpO2 trends en route.
