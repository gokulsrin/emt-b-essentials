## Text

<b>SCI complete</b>: {{c1::total loss of motor and sensory function below the injury level::deficit}}.<br><b>SCI incomplete</b>: {{c2::some function preserved below the injury level::deficit}}.

## Extra

Any residual function below the lesion (movement, sensation, sacral sparing) defines an incomplete injury and carries a better prognosis. In the field, document PMS in all four extremities and perineal sensation when possible.
