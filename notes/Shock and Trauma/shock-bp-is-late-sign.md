## Text

In <b>shock</b>, hypotension is a {{c1::late::early or late?}} sign; by the time BP drops, an adult has already lost roughly {{c2::30%::% blood volume lost}} of blood volume.

## Extra

Treat shock based on mechanism plus mental status and skin signs, not the BP cuff. Pediatric patients compensate vigorously with heart rate and crash with less warning than adults.
