## Text

<u>Hives</u> + <u>airway swelling</u> + hypotension → suspect {{c1::anaphylactic::shock type}} shock.

## Extra

Anaphylaxis is a distributive shock triggered by an allergen (Hymenoptera stings, foods, medications, latex). Epinephrine IM is the first-line treatment. The airway threat (bronchospasm, angioedema) makes this a higher-priority airway emergency than other shock types.
