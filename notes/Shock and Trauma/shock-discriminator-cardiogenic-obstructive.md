## Text

<u>Wet lung sounds</u> + <u>JVD</u> with hypotension → suspect {{c1::cardiogenic::shock type}} or {{c1::obstructive::shock type}} shock.

## Extra

Both cardiogenic (pump failure) and obstructive (tension pneumothorax, tamponade, massive PE) can produce JVD and wet lungs from fluid backing up. Key differentiator: obstructive causes are traumatic/mechanical (look for unilateral absent breath sounds, deviated trachea, muffled heart sounds); cardiogenic usually has a cardiac history.
