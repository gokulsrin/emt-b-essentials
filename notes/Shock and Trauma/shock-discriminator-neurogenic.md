## Text

<u>Warm, dry, pink</u> skin with hypotension and bradycardia → suspect {{c1::neurogenic::shock type}} shock.

## Extra

Loss of sympathetic tone below the spinal injury means peripheral vessels dilate and the heart cannot compensate with tachycardia. Contrast with most other shock types, which present with cool, clammy skin and tachycardia.
