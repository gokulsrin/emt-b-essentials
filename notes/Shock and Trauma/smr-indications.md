## Text

<b>Spinal motion restriction (SMR)</b> is indicated when any of the following are present:
{{c1::altered mental status}}, {{c1::spinal pain or midline tenderness}}, {{c1::focal neurologic deficit}}, {{c1::anatomic spinal deformity}}, {{c1::distracting injury}}, {{c1::high-risk MOI}}.

## Extra

High-risk MOI examples: high-speed MVC, fall > 3x patient's height, axial load, diving injury.
Distracting injury: a painful injury severe enough that the patient may not reliably report back pain (e.g., an open femur fracture).
All six indications apply to the conscious, cooperative patient; altered mental status alone mandates SMR.
