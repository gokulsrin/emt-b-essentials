## Text

Modern <b>SMR</b> sequence: {{c1::manual stabilization → cervical collar → secure to stretcher::steps}}; long backboards are reserved for {{c2::extrication or unstable spines::specific indication}}, not every potential C-spine call.

## Extra

Routine long-board immobilization for every potential C-spine patient is no longer the default in most EMS systems. Allow patients to self-extricate when feasible. Document a focused neuro exam (PMS x4 extremities) before and after any movement.
