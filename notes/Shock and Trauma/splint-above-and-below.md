## Text

When splinting a fracture, immobilize the joint {{c1::above and below::relative to fracture site}} the injury site.

## Extra

Immobilizing only one adjacent joint allows the other to pivot and displace the fracture. Always assess and document PMS (pulse, motor, sensory) distal to the injury before and after splint application.
