## Text

<b>Sternal fracture</b> raises concern for {{c1::cardiac contusion::cardiac injury}} and {{c1::aortic injury::vascular injury}}.

## Extra

A high-energy anterior chest strike (steering wheel) is the classic mechanism. ECG changes (arrhythmias, ST changes) may indicate myocardial contusion. Widened mediastinum on CXR suggests aortic injury — rapid transport is critical.
