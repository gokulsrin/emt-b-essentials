<b>Strain</b> = {{c1::muscle or tendon::tissue type}} injury; <b>Sprain</b> = {{c2::ligament::tissue type}} injury.

## Extra

Both are treated with RICE (rest, ice, compression, elevation) prehospitally. Neither can be definitively differentiated from a fracture in the field — if in doubt, splint.
