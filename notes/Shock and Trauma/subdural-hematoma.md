## Text

<b>Subdural hematoma</b>: venous bleed ({{c1::bridging veins::vessel}}); typical presentation is {{c2::gradual decline::course}}, most common in {{c2::elderly or alcoholic patients::population}}.

## Extra

Crescent-shaped on CT (follows brain contour). Chronic SDH can occur weeks after a minor head injury. Elderly and alcoholics have cortical atrophy with longer, more vulnerable bridging veins.
