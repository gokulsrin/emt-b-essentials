## Text

<b>Open pneumothorax</b> (sucking chest wound): a chest wall defect that allows {{c1::air to move freely between the pleural space and the atmosphere::mechanism}} with each breath.

## Extra

You may hear air "sucking" and see bubbling, frothy blood at the wound. Air follows the path of least resistance — if the wound is large enough, more air enters through it than through the trachea on each breath.
