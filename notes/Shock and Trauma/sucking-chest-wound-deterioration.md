## Text

If a patient with a sealed <b>sucking chest wound</b> deteriorates (rising distress, hypotension, <u>JVD</u>), {{c1::lift the dressing to release trapped air::action}}.

## Extra

Sealing a sucking chest wound can convert it to a tension pneumothorax if air continues to leak from the lung into the pleural space. Reassess constantly after any occlusive dressing is applied. Lifting/burping the seal is the EMT-level intervention; needle decompression is paramedic-scope.
