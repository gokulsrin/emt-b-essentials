## Text

Treatment of a <b>sucking chest wound</b>: apply a commercial vented chest seal, or an <u>occlusive dressing taped on three sides</u>, leaving {{c1::one side unsealed::which side}} as a flutter valve.

## Extra

Three-sided dressings allow air to escape during expiration (pressure pushes the open edge up) but prevent air from entering during inspiration (pressure seals the edge down). Commercial vented seals do this more reliably on sweaty or bloody chest walls. High-flow O2; BVM if ventilation is inadequate.
