## Text

Late field findings of <b>tension pneumothorax</b>: <u>JVD</u> and <u>tracheal deviation</u> {{c1::away from::direction}} the affected side.

## Extra

Early findings are non-specific (respiratory distress, hypoxia, tachycardia, hypotension, AMS, diminished breath sounds on the affected side, hyperresonance on percussion). JVD and tracheal deviation are late and often missed in the field. Absent breath sounds unilaterally is the most reliable early discriminator.
