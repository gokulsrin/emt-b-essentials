## Text

<b>Tension pneumothorax</b>: air enters the pleural space and {{c1::cannot escape::direction of flow}}; pressure builds, collapses the lung, shifts the mediastinum, and produces {{c2::obstructive shock::shock type}}.

## Extra

The mediastinal shift kinks the great vessels, reducing venous return to the right heart and dropping cardiac output. This is why tension PTX kills — it is primarily a cardiovascular failure, not purely a respiratory one.
