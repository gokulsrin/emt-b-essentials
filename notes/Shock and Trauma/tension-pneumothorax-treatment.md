## Text

EMT-level treatment of suspected <b>tension pneumothorax</b> from a sealed chest wound: {{c1::lift one corner of the occlusive dressing::intervention}} to release trapped air, then rapid transport with ALS intercept.

## Extra

Needle decompression is paramedic-scope, not EMT-B. "Burping" the chest seal is the one direct intervention available to most EMT-Bs. If the tension PTX is not from a sealed wound (blunt trauma, spontaneous), the EMT can only provide O2/BVM support and expedite ALS/transport.
