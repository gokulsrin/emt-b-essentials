## Text

Never place a <b>tourniquet</b> {{c1::over a joint (knee or elbow)::location to avoid}}; move proximal to the next bone segment.

## Extra

Joints are bony prominences with irregular surfaces — a tourniquet placed over a joint cannot generate uniform circumferential pressure to occlude the artery.
