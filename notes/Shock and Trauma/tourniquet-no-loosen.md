## Text

Once a <b>tourniquet</b> is applied in the field, do {{c1::not loosen or remove it::action to avoid}}; only definitive care removes it.

## Extra

Loosening releases the clot, causes re-bleeding, and can precipitate a fatal reperfusion injury. Prehospital removal is not indicated regardless of patient request or apparent bleeding cessation.
