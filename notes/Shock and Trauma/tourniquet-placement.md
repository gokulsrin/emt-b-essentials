## Text

A <b>tourniquet</b> is placed {{c1::2–3 inches proximal to the wound::position}}, on bare skin, on a single bone segment when possible.

## Extra

Placing on bare skin and over a single bone (not over clothing or padding) ensures adequate pressure transmission and arterial occlusion.
