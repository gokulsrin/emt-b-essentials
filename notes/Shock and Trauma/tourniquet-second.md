## Text

If one <b>tourniquet</b> fails to stop bleeding, apply a {{c1::second tourniquet just proximal to the first::action}}.

## Extra

Do not remove or reposition the first tourniquet — it may be partially occluding and disturbing it can worsen bleeding. The second tourniquet provides additional pressure at a slightly higher level. Document both application times.
