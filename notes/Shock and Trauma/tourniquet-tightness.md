## Text

Tighten a <b>tourniquet</b> until {{c1::bleeding stops}} AND {{c2::distal pulse disappears::pulse finding}}.

## Extra

Both endpoints are required. Stopping visible bleeding without obliterating the distal pulse means arterial flow is still present and the tourniquet is inadequate. A "too loose" tourniquet can actually increase venous bleeding.
