## Text

After applying a <b>tourniquet</b>, mark the {{c1::time of application::what to mark}} clearly on the tourniquet <u>and</u> on the patient (e.g., "TK 1432").

## Extra

Time documentation is critical because tourniquet time guides the receiving surgeon's decisions about limb viability and reperfusion management. Mark it in permanent marker on the tourniquet itself and on the patient's skin or forehead. Verbally report it during handoff.
