## Text

<b>Traction splint</b> contraindications: {{c1::knee or hip injury}}, {{c1::lower-leg or ankle injury}}, {{c1::suspected pelvic fracture}}, {{c1::partial amputation}}.

## Extra

Applying traction across a knee, hip, or pelvic injury dramatically worsens displacement and hemorrhage. For partial amputations, traction can complete the amputation and destroy remaining neurovascular structures.
