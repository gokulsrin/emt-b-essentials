## Text

<b>Traction splint</b> indication: {{c1::isolated mid-shaft femur fracture::specific injury}}.

## Extra

Traction realigns the fracture, reduces muscle spasm, decreases the thigh compartment space, and limits internal hemorrhage. Not indicated for any other injury — contraindications include knee/hip involvement, lower-leg or ankle injury, pelvic fracture, and partial amputation.
